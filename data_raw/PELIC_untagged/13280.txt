There are many important things to consider when renting an apartment. The main factor is the location. The monthly rent also matters. A tenant should know if the rent includes utilities. If one need a new apartment quickly it is important to know when it will be available. The are a few things about the interior. It must be considered how new appliances are and how big the closets are. A view from the windows is an important factor too. It is wise to ask whether there is a laundry in the basement. Thus a careful tenant should take many important factors into account when renting a new apartment.
