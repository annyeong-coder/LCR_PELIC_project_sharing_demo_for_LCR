This text is talking about the similarity between two different jobs coaches and business manager. The first similarity is the task of leading the team or employees. Both of them train their groups. Second one is to solve problems in the group. Both of them play the role of intermediaries. Finally the last similarity is both of them must represent their subordinates to the members of higher management. They should report all information about everyone in the group to the owners. In conclusion the two jobs look like completely different but the roles are really related.
