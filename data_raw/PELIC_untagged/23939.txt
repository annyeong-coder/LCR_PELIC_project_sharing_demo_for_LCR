Barber chef by profession but an expert on agriculture and healthy and good tasted food discloses in this TED conference a new way of fish raising. It could be the solution for environmental problems that humankind is facing. In this conference Barber explains giving as example an amazing auto sufficient farm in Spain how the creation of natural farms based on ecological relationships is possible. This new model might be the solution to current challenges on food supply. Therefore barber opens the debate about how human should produce their own food and the environmental impact that this activity is currently having due to pesticide use contamination and soil destruction.
