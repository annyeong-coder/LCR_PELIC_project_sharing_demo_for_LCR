Nowadays in a period of deep job shortage anxiety is the most common psychological problem people have. Complex combination of negative emotions like fear apprehension and concern the anxiety is often accompanied by physical sensations like palpitations chest pain short breath and nausea. The motivating factors of anxiety can be manifold health problems family problems personal problems and economical problems. The first cause that can provoke an anxious state is represented by health problems the discovery of a disease or a delicate state of health has as a consequence the arising of a mental distress. For example a doctor diagnoses a cancer to a mother of 2 kids. Consequently if she has not a strong character a sensation of fear and anxiety could win the day on her. Furthermore economical problems are other causes of anxiety. For instance the lack of the money in front of a big purchase like a house or having an occasional job with the risk to be fired are the most common situations that people are living in these days. In Italy for example there were a lot of cases where several entrepreneurs committed suicide because the banks deny them loans provoking a anxious state to them not to find a solution.
