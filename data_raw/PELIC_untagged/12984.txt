On the surface coaches and business managers are different however these contrasts pale in comparison to the likeness of main functions of the two professions. A primary task of both coaches and business managers is to lead the team members. The coaches are responsible for solving problems between players likewise the managers are in charge of dealing with employees' trouble. Not only coaches but also managers are representatives of their subordinates in order to report to the higher management. As we have seen the fundamentals of both occupations are to improve communication and develop relationships between other members or employees.
