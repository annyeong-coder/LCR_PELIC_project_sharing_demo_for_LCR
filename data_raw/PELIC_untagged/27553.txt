Summary The Psychic Detective The article describes about how the psychic uses power to help the police find the missing people. Annette Martin has a psychic ability when she was little and then she becomes the police assistant after conventional survey fails. Martin has joined in many cases with police agencies. A retired detective Rick Keaton deeply puts his belief on Martin's successful deeds however Joe Nickell has different opinion from the detective. He thinks that psychic power is just more guesses imagination and emotion than scientific detection.
