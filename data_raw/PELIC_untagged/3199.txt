ChanHo Park has been baseball player in major league for many years. He has gone to the mound about 270 games since 1994. He has won about 110 games since 1994. Also he has received gold medal for 10 years. He has made a lot of money since 1994. He has managed ChanHo Company in Korea since 2001. He has been hero and rich person since he debuted as baseball player.
