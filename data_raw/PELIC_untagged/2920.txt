Indent Cathedral of Learning in the campus of the University of Pittsburgh is like a giraffe in the middle of the zoo. The Cathedral of Learning is located in the heart of the campus of the University. This building like the giraffe in the zoo is the most admired attraction in the city of Pittsburgh because of his beauty and exceptionality. Cathedral which has 42 levels and measures 535 feet is the tallest building in the campus of the University of Pittsburgh and the second tallest education building in the world. Furthermore the Cathedral of Learning is surrounded by other important edifices of the University however their architecture isn't more attractive than the marvelous design and style of the Cathedral. Moreover as only the eyes of the giraffe can see the rest of the animals the Cathedral offers an exclusive and beautiful view from four sides of the building at the top 38th level where you can observe all the University campus and some places of the gorgeous city of Pittsburgh. Finally the Cathedral of Learning like the giraffe in the zoo can't be ignored by tourists and Pittsburghers because of the strategic location extraordinary height and unique architecture.
