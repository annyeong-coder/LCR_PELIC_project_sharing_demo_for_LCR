Consider Three Four Five Factors when planning a family vacation There are many factors should be considered when planning a family vacation. First agree on location when agreeing on location trip planner should consider the interests of each family member in that location. Second familiarize yourself and your family about the selected location by visiting tourist websites and the selected city's official tour guide. Third make all reservations in advance such as airline tickets and hotel bookings in order to avoid unavailability and peak rate. Fourth do all the necessary travel and medical insurance. Fifth apply for a tourist visa if your destination requires a visa. Some visas such as European visas Schengen require medical insurances confirmed round trip tickets and confirmed hotel booking to be submitted along with a visa application. Finally take all the necessary food and medicine with you. To sum up trip planner should do all the possible efforts in order to avoid all the expected barriers and make a successful vacation.
