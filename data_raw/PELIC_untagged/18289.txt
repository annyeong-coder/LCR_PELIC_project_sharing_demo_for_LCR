The number of foreign students at Springfield Academy is growing every year. First between the years 1992 and 1996 the number of American students was more than the foreign students. In 1996 the number of American students was the same as the foreign students. After 1996 the foreign students were growing fast. Between the years 1996 and 2002 the foreign students increased three times. There are two messages. First Springfield Academy is a popular school for the foreign students. Second the school needs to offer more social and cultural activities for the foreign students. The foreign students are not only studying but they can also become aware of American culture. It will be helpful to their studies.
