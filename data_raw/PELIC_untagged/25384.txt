People have started to use the herbs and spices thousands years ago. The main source of herbs includes the green leaf plants and the source of spices includes the plant parts. The herbs and spices can be used both in enriching the tastes and enhancing the health status. For example Tumeric a kind of herb plant can be both used to perfect the taste of food and used to help the cure of the cancer. Also other herbs and spices such as garlic and ginger can help to prevent the cancer cinnamon can help to reduce blood sugar. Because these feature of them the herbs and spices were often became the center goods of trading in ancient time and the causes of the wars. Nowadays the herbs and spices are elaborately researched and widely used in the field of medicine by the scientists and researchers.
