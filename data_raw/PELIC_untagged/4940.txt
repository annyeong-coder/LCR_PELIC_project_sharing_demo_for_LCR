Thesis Statement There are three effective ways to manage time. 1. Making a realistic schedule. A. Make a schedule day by day. B. Write a schedule on the paper and check frequently. C. Write a schedule in the order that it is important. 2. Using short time which can be easily wasted. A. Read helpful books on the bus or train. B. Use the remaining lunchtime after having lunch. 3. Reducing unnecessary waste of time. A. Reduce time which is spent playing a game. B. Foster helpful habits. A. Finish the work you should do in any case. B. Get up and go to bed at a regular time.
