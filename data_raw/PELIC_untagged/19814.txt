If you ever have gone to a Chinese restaurant you might have a chance to taste fried rice. It is a colorful dish with white rice red shrimp or ham yellow egg and green peas. In fact it came from my hometown Yangzhou City in China. In this article I am going to introduce Yangzhou Cuisine and some typical food in Pittsburgh. My topics will cover Yangzhou Fried Rice Fuchun Baozi and Gan Si from my city and Big Mac Pierogies and Chipped Ham from Pittsburgh.
