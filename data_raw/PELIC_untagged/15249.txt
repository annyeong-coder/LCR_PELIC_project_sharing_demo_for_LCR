Clean air is essential to our lives. Governments and People need to cooperate in order to ensure better air quality. The clean air is believed to be as important as clean water. The cleaner the air the healthier the people will be. When we say clean air we mean the air that is free of CO2. The CO2 is a toxic compound that our bodies always trying to get rid of. Unfortunately this toxic material is released into the air by industries. The only way to decrease is to plant trees. A tree can help saving our environment as trees are considered the lungs of nature.
