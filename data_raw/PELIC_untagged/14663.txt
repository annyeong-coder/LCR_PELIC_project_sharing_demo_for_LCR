In A Chimp off the Old Block Discover magazine January 2002 the author says that Ayumu which is chimp prodigy can match Japanese characters to colored shapes on a computer screen. In February researchers discovered that Ayumu learned them by observing his mother . Ayumu s mother has learned to read several dozen characters and can match written words to color but Ayumu had never touched the screen before researches discovered him skill. Researchers are hopeful that Ayumu will continue learn by observing his mother without researchers helping.
