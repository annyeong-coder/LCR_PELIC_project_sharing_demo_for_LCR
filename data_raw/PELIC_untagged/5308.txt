Every subjects which consists of reading writing grammar listening speaking in ELI has authentic practices to improve specific skills in learning English. In reading classes we practice how to summarize articles from newspaper magazines or any other books by outside reading. We learn new vocabularies in those texts as well. In writing classes we learn about essay's formation and practice to write many types of essays. In grammar classes we don't only learn variety grammar but also practice uses of grammar in common conversation by GRS which is programmed for recording answers of general questions. In speaking classes we learn useful expression and applications are checked everyday. RSA program is providing checking instrument by record and dictation of our short presentations.
