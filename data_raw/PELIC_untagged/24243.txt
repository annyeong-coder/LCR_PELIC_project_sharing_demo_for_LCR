TOEFL Test International students who want to study at any American university must to do TOEFL test and get a specific score to be eligible to enter the university. Most of universities in U. S. Require this score on TOEFL to accept any international student. Therefore many international students have some argument about why they need to do this test while other American students don't. But TOEFL test has organized well to improve the student's abilities in English and they need to do it to be able to study in any American university. TOEFL test is a necessary test to determine the student who can enter the university because it indicates student's abilities in English that let him enter a university. American universities teach in English and if a student doesn't have enough skills in English he can't do well and he is going to fail. TOEFL test is prepared by special institutes and has many sections that examine the student's abilities in reading writing grammar listening and speaking. Student should prepare to this test and take many tests to get the score that the university wants.
