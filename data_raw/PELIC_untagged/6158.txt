Here are some tips that you have to do when you going to write a paragraphs. First you have to think about your topic and organize your ideas. Second you can use a brainstorming to make a list as many ideas as possible without worring about how you will use them. Also you can use a clustering technique. It is a visual way of showing how your ideas are connected using circles and lines. In addition you have to use your ideas to write a first draft. This step is called writing. Finally you have to improving what you have written. This step is called revising.
