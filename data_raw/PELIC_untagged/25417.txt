It was a little while before Margot made her steps out of the closet. As she walked out she was staring steadily at the ground. She moved slowly passed the children. After a couple of steps she stopped in silence for a moment then suddenly turned her face to look at them. They immediately avoided her eyes and hung their heads. Margot moved her eyes away from them looked briefly through the widow and walked away silently as if nothing has happened at all.
