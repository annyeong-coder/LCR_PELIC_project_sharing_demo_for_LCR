This article talks about description of the sense of brotherhood sister experienced in liver transplant. Lisa was donor and Christina was transplant recipient who suffered from hereditary amyloidosis. Their mother was died for same Christina's disease. Sometimes the operation is at risk for complications but Lisa made a decision about it. Christina accepted liver as a gift from Lisa. The operation was success. They could both feel their deep love for each other.
