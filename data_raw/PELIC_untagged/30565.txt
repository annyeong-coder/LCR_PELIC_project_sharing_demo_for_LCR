The article is about what are the history problem and recent situation of the immigrant of America. The immigrants usually came to America to search a better life because of the economic problems in their hometown. According to that they were often look down by Americans and had a hard physical or dangerous work. Besides although the new immigrants needed to change their original beliefs to embrace the new life they still tried hard to keep their own culture language and religious practices until now. Owing to the various and intercultural influences the United States has been described a melting pot. Therefore the immigrant heritage is an important part of American culture.
