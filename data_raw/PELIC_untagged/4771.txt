When you plan to travel you should prepare schedule of a trip for you. First of all you should choose who will go with you. It helps you to save money and also you can have fun with your friends. Second you should decide where you want to go. In other words which cities are better or which places are interesting for you For example if you go to Pittsburgh you probably should go to Mount Washington to see the sunsets because it's so beautiful. Third you should reserve transportation and a room in a hotel. If you surf the internet to make the reservation you can find the cheapest hotel. It makes you waste money when you don't. Finally you have to think about how much money you will need If so you can be prepared for an emergency. A trip especially a foreign trip is a danger without preparation. If you could know what you need for the trip you would be perfect traveler.
