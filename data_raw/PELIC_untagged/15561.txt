Pudim is a delicious dessert from Brazil. It is easy to make if you follow these simples steps. For you cook a great pudim you will need some ingredient. First you need pudim ingredients. There are 2 cans of condensed milk 2 cans of milk use the same cans from the condensed milk for measurements and 5 eggs. Second you need caramel ingredients. There are 1 cup of castor sugar 1 2 cup of water. You should start with the caramel. First bring the water and sugar in a small pan. You need to boiling it in a medium heat on the stove. Then when it become liquid and dark brown it mean the caramel is ready. Second put the caramel inside the bunt baking pan. Next set it is pudim put all the ingredients together the eggs milk condensed milk in a blender. Blend it on high for 5 minutes. It will become a liquid. After that put the liquid inside the same bunt baking pan. Then put the bunt pan on the water in the deep baking pan. After that you put it inside the oven and let it for 1 hour and 40 minutes. When it ready you should wait 2 hour to take it out the burn baking pan. Then you take it out and put in the refrigerator. It is a nice and delicious dessert from Brazil. You should try and enjoy it.
