The main functions of athletic team coaches and business managers are very close. First the most fundamental similarity between these two jobs is the task of leading the team members or employees. Both occupations should give the appropriate directions based on the ability of players or employees. Second the both two professions is required the ability to solve problems between teammates or employees. The incorporation between athletes or employees sometimes triggers to the bad results so coaches or business managers must quiet down their discord. Third both coaches and managers must represent their subordinates to the members of higher management. This means both two occupations often be the links between their supervisor and the lower employees. In conclusion the job description of athletic team coaches and business managers is very related because both occupations are basically improving communication and understanding of others.
