Passing the TOFEL test requires a lot of energy and dedication. Energy because you have to start your preparation months before your due date. If you are taking English courses you should also study Tofel preparation books which include ways and samples of questions to practice. Experts advise students to start four month ahead of schedule for at least four hours a day. Taking the TOFEL needs a busy study schedule and a change in daily routine. That is why a lot of students postpone this test as much as they can because they can't make this hard decision. Another important aspect is dedication which is very important in passing the TOFEL because TOFEL test contains many parts such as reading writing speaking listening and vocabulary. Therefore in order to feel ready for the test you should cover all of these parts and be ready to answer similar questions. This dedication in time and effort will be worth it when a student gets the score he she desires. Of Course all of this effort will be a memory the minute you see your passing score.
