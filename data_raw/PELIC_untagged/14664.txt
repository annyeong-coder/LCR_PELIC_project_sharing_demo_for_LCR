According to the article A chimp off the old block Ayumu ten months old chimpanzee is a prodigy who demonstrates his literary skills by matching Japanese characters to colored shape on a computer screen. He correctly matched the kanji word for brown with a brown square to earn 100 yen for buy some raisins from a vending machine. He is a son of Ai a 25 years old chimpanzee who had learned to read several dozen characters in Kanji a form written Japanese. She likes to match the words to colors and shapes to earn 100 yen coins to by some snacks. She is one of a celebrity due to her mental prowess at the Kyoto University Primate Research Institute in Japan. The researchers discovered that Ayumu may be teaching himself how to read and they also hope that Ayumu will continue to lean simply by observing his mother without having to be coached.
