There are a lot of interesting things to see and do in New York city. It is home of over 150 world class museums. There are art museums science museums photography museums natural history museums and even a museum of sport history. New York is known for their rich variety of theater music and dance. From the bright lights of Broadway and the respected stages at Lincoln Center and Carnegie Hall to the high kicks of the rockettes at Radio City music hall and incredible jazz at intimate clubs which are something for everyone. Many people go to New York for the wonderful restaurants. There are thousands of restaurants to please every palate and wallet. If you are looking for a place to shop. You will find everything that you can imagine. With more than 10 000 shops filled with brand names and bargains from around the world. NYC is a shopper's paradise. As for me New York pastime is my favorite people watching
