The Proposal is a comedy movie that describes a very randomlove story that happens between an assistant and his boss. The assistant always wanted to be an author while his boss limited him from his options. She was not a US citizen and when she was going ot be deported she assumed that she is married to him. To make that part of reality she made him take her to his parents house. His parents love her although they don't know the plan and force them to get married. They both decide to do it so they can make them happy. At the wedding she admits that it was only a plan so she doesnt travel. After that she gets ready to travel back to her home country but he stops her because he is crazily in love with her and she is too. So they decide to not stick to the plan and actually be in a relationship and get married and she gets to say in the country too. They live happily ever after.
