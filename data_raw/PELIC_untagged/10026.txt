In Italy a controversial and animated dispute involves the environment issue nuclear energy. The debate got more intense after the 1987 referendum in which Italians subsequent to the 1986 Cernobyl accident decided to close every nuclear power station nationwide. Nowadays the question has being getting urgent because of the Italian increasing energy requirements. Most nuclear energy is imported from foreign countries expecially from France. It means a great expense and yet a great many Italians are still unwilling to hold nuclear power stations or radioactive waste depots on their territory.
