Big archaeology find in Egypt. A University of Chicago expedition at Tell Edfu in southern Egypt has unearthed fresh clues about the emergence of urban life. It will be new information about ancient Egypt. Nowadays Due to intense farming and heavy settlement over the years much of the record of urban civilization has been lost. For this reason the work at Edfu is important and innovative in that it finally allows us to examine ancient Egypt as an urban society. At Tell Edfu archaeologists have uncovered what amount to a downtown area. It is exactly at this period 1773 1650 B. C. When Edfu seems to have been very prosperous.
