This article talk about chill pepper. Chill pepper is the fruit of the plants from the genus capsicum. Chill pepper culinary use is generally as a spice. It is now grown around the world because it is widely used as spices or vegetables in cuisine and as medicine. We can find easily chill pepper that is several of dishes the entire world. It has a hot flavor that we may feel bite the tongue or fiery taste. Also capsaicin derived from chili pepper has a great relieving effect.
