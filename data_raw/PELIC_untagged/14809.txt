What are the benefits of having pets Recently there are many families that have only one child. In this case the child could feel lonely because they have no siblings to be with. By having pets children feel satisfaction with their pets. They can learn responsibility about pet. As like parents children pay attention and take care of it. While children have a good time with pets they feel fun of course and learn about the way to control their feeling. Additionally not only having pets provide the good for mind but also it's good for health. People receive more chance to walking or jogging because being with pets is not boring. For some people who have problem with mind pets can be their partner or friend. It's helpful for someone who has depression or lonely.
