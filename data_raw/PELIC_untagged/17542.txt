Climate is not the same as weather happening just a short period of time. Categories of climates depend on their location on the earth. Sun's ray is the most effect to climates that depend on angle of the sun's ray. Temperature regions are farther from the equator. The other factors of climates are winds mountains and oceans. Winds bring moisture to land that produce rain or thunderstorm. Mountains force wind to rise and cause moisture to condense in clouds and rain. Oceans provide moisture for rain. Classification of climate are Tropical Steppes and Deserts Midlatitude Continental Polar and High elevation climates.
