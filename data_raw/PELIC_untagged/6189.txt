A Rattletrap East German Icon Has Its Day Again Produced until 1991 and symbolized former East Germany Trabant has had a symbolic meaning for 50 years. Trabant owners celebrated the car's 50th anniversary in Zwickau Germany. This meeting has drawn visitors from other countries. The Trabant is a symbol that is a link to the past and a small gesture of resistance against the prosperity and luxury living standards. Trabant is also an example of romantic pluck than of Communist ineptness. Although it is produced a lot of luxury cars in Germany Trabant preserves its appeal.
