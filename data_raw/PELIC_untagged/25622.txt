Water is the most important for living things. People must drink water and fish must stay in rivers lakes or seas. However Water have been polluting by garbage industrial sewage oil spilling and so on. For example the newest oil spilling in the Gulf of Mexico not only has made marine lives at risk but also has threatened to the range of the gulf coast food stocks. Experts and scientists have been working on the solutions of this environmental issue but until now the fact that the quality of water are getting worse and worse has caused people fearing to consume fish. How can we keep water cleaning That is a big question that everybody should think about.
