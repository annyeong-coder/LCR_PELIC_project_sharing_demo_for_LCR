What is a good paragraph A good paragraph is composed of four important factors such as correct grammar intelligible organization specific expression and effective content to reader. The first factor is that using correct grammar in a paragraph is the base of writing because it's necessary for readers to understand with accuracy. The next factor is that using intelligible organization in a paragraph is the function of helping the arrangement for readers. In other words a good organized paragraph is necessary for readers to easily understand. The third factor is that using specific expression in a paragraph is to support author's idea. In other words a good paragraph includes reasons examples evident and details for readers to understand easily. Finally using an effective content in a paragraph is the most important factor. If a paragraph makes readers happy impressive and stirring the paragraph must be an effective and a good. I think that a good paragraph is not only to be composed correct grammar intelligible organization and specific expression but also to give readers a good influence.
