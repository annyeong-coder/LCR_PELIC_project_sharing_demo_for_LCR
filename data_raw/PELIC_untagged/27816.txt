Alcohol consumption in the U. S has been on the rise each year. Also the problems related to alcohol are getting increase. Especially since The National Minimum Drinking Age Act of 1984 was passed on July 17 1984 by the United States Congress underage drinking problems have been a controversial issue. Legal drinking age in U. S. Is twenty one years old. However some people argue that the government should lower the age of legal drinking to eighteen because prohibition of alcohol for young adult might result in side effect such as irresponsible drinking.
