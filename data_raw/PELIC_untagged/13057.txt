This article wrote about the similarities between athlete's coaches and business managers. Fist both coaches and manager have the same responsibility manage or organize the team members. For example they need to encourage and convince their teams in order to achieve the goals. Second not only coaches need the ability to solve problems but also managers do. It is inevitable that the coaches and managers must to solve the facing problem except the problem that bigger than their authority. Last coaches have to reveal team's movements to broad just as managers do. For instance the coach usually reports the big boss about the team who were the good player or bad player. On the other hand the manager also does weekly or monthly reports.
