The story talks about Hannah and his prodigal brother Ralph . He escaped from his family to make wealth. Years passed his parents were died but Hannah was waiting him. She was taking care of her garden in the isolated house. She was lonely. Most people believed her brother wad died or would come back poor. Whereas she believed he would come back and be a rich man. One day Ralph came back poor and cripple. All the people knew about him. They thought she would be sad because he wasn't rich. On the contrary she was proud of him and believes rich isn't crucial the rich in love and happiness is the main thing.
