Serge Rachmaninoff Foundation The Serge Rachmaninoff Foundation was organized at 1999. By Serge Rachmaninoff's grandson Alexander Rachmaninoff. This Foundation not only support people who loves music and performed the great works by Serge Rachmaninoff. Also they support other composer's repertoires such like Prokofiev Scriabin and so on. Through this foundation many of exllent artists performed in the music hall or festivals everywhere. Those artists also bring the wonderful music all over the world.
