The main point of The Stereotype of Stereotypes is just showing us two different point of view about stereotypes. It introduces the positive and the negative opinions about stereotypes. On the other hand in this article the author says that personalities reflecting national or cultural stereotypes do not have any relationship with the real personality they measured. This is not about whether stereotypes are positive or not but about whether stereotypes especially in the aspect of cultural or national personality are scientifically reasonable or not.
