According to travel statistics more and more people in Taiwan would like to go to Korea instead of Japan for their vacation. People who choose the location for visit will consider the price weather and food. Therefore eating has become the most important thing. First of all eating habits in Taiwan and Korea are very similar. People who live in both two countries eat rice as their staple. They always have rice in lunch and dinner but sometimes they have it in breakfast especially for old people who had the bad teeth would like to eat soup with rice. However they have the same dishes but use different ways to cook. For example Kim chi in Korea is red salty and spicy and made form Chinese cabbage Kim chi in Taiwan is white sour and sweet and made from cabbage. Although they look alike but actually is not the same one. No matter how eating habits in Taiwan or Korea is alike or not concededly it is the most important thing.
