In the age of computer finding a book in a library has become very easy. First of all find the computers that contain the library's catalogs . There are two catalogs one for books and one for periodicals . In the book catalog type the topic you are seeking information about in the space labeled subject . Then Scroll through the entries for the books that are displayed on the computer screen and determine which ones seem the most relevant . After that write down the title and call number of each book that you want . Locate the books on the library shelves by their call numbers. Be prepared to show your library card at the checkout desk when you take the books to the checkout desk.
