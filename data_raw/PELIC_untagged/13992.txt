Let me show you how to cook Castella . Ingredients you need are eggs sugar strong flour milk honey and oil. First beat egg whites until they peaks. Second add sugar to them and stir well. After that add yolks strong flour milk honey oil to them and stir well in case each too. When they are glossy pour them into a mold. And then bake them in the oven for forty minutes. Finally remove them from the mold put them upside down and let them cool. This cooking method makes you a expert at Castella .
