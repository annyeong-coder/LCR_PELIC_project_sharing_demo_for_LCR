Technology is like an effective medicine which can not only provide solutions but also make some side effects for us. In order to seek much better life humans have been developing technology and getting abundant achievements for a long time. The highly developed technology not only brings us the convenient and extended life but also influences our behaviors and living environment. First our lifestyle changed a lot because the high tech electric products had been invented. Besides the world is smaller and smaller due to high speed transportations are developed. For example we can receive the latest news by TV or internets communicate with others directly by cell phones and travel around the world rapidly by high speed planes or trains. Therefore we can own the comfortable and convenient life because the distance between us is much shorter than before. In addition human life had been longer and extended by the developed technology and well medicine. The average life span of human has grown up to 78 years in the U. S. In 2011. The United Nations also expected that the population of the world would increase to seven billion until 2012. What caused this result The developed technology make the medicine be progressive and the food plant massively. Nevertheless there are also some problems accompany by highly developed technology. The most serious is that Global Warming has made a huge impact and let some species become extinct. The unusual climate has also brought about some disaster and claimed inestimable lives. Developed technology may not only bring us positive effects but also negative influences. It all depends on our attitudes! Therefore the government policies and strategies should be modified in time according to the high developed technology in order to provide the suitable environment for citizens and protect our own Earth.
