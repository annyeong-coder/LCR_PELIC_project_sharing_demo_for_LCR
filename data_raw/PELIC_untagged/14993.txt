A comparison between Jim Springer and Jim Lewis demonstrates extraordinary similarities of habits. First of all when feeling nervous both of them act on same way. Jim Springer older brother bites his fingernails and jiggles his foot when scared. Likewise his brother Jim Lewis eats his nails and shakes his legs if he is fearful. Moreover Jim Springer is same the walking way as Jim Lewis. Usually Jim Lewis steps on right feet at first like Jim Springer. Besides walking way they use the same gestures when they are speaking. Jin springer shows his palms or holds his hands strongly and Jim Lewis does too. Although they were born at the same time and from same mother they have too much similar common abnormally.
