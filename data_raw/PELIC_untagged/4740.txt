There are many things to do when you want to have a weekend trip to another city. First you have to gathering information about the city where you want to go. For example you have to find some interested places or special foods of that city and you have to check the weather on that day that you want to go. Then you should plan about the places that you will go and a time to go there. Next if you want to go by your own car you must make sure that your car can reach that city without a problem. You should check all wheels engine and wipers that they already work. Moreover you have to prepare a budget for this trip. This is very important step because you should not want to travel without money. Finally you have to pack necessary things such as cloths and cooking equipments for camping or picnic . You may prepare a first aid for an accident. In conclusion if you do following by these checklist you will have a fantastic weekend trip.
