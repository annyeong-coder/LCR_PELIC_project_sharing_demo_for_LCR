To improve your listening skills in English these are several ways to help you. First of all you should listen to music as you can. Second you should listen to radio. Next Find out how to switch languages on your TV. If you have digital or satellite TV there are several channels that broadcast in multiple languages. In addition listening whilst reading is a good idea there are lots of audio books on the market. Then you try to speak with an American speaker or make many Americans friends. At this point all these help you to memorize a lot of vocabulary to help you when you are listening. At last all these exercises are good but you should continue do these to improve your skills.
