Sleep Apnea is a common sleeping disorder and means without breath . Sufferers of sleep apnea stop breathing repeatedly while they sleep. There are three different types of sleep apnea obstructive sleep apnea central sleep apnea and mixed apnea. It associated with a number of risk factors including being overweight male and over the age of forty. It diagnoses by sending patients to a sleep center for a polysomnography test and in extreme cases surgery is needed to make a clear passage for the air.
