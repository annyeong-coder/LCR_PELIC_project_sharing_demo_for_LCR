Title of Article Try to move on Author Col. Julio Lopez Source The Times Pennsylvania Date march 11 2010 Last February 27 th Chile had an enormous earthquake. It was of magnitude of 8. 8 On the Richter scale it was in the center of the their country. It was 500 times stronger than the earthquake in Haiti. Chile is advanced in technology and build but anyway the buildings in that area had structural damage. Step by step Chileans try to continue with their lives. The president said on TV that she would help everybody what needs help. She also said that she felt really frustrated because she worked to base completely in disinformation but now government navy and another department are working together. Many people are helping in different areas trying to move Chile again. Vocabulary 1. Ferry verb Distance from on place to another in a boat or other vehicle. 2. Countless adj Too many to be counted.
