The Giver written by Lois Lowry is the story of the community that can be described as 'an ideal'. People were living with perfect life no pain risk and ambiguity. Everything in the community was perfectly planned by decision making of the Committee of Elders. When Jonas turned twelve he was given the assignment as Receiver of Memory form the Giver who had memories form the past. The memories are prohibited to people because they lead to the pain. The climax of the story was how his father releases a twin. When Jonas knew the truth behind the perfect life and Gabriel whom Jonas was taking care of was determined to be released he decided to leave the community with Gabriel.
