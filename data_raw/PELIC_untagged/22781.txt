Central do Brasil starring ANON_NAME_0 Montenegro is a drama telling the story about a friendship between an old woman and a boy. The boy had lost his mother in a bus accident and became homeless. The woman working at the Rio de Janeiro Central Station is frustrated about her job writing letters for illiteracy people there. After the boy lost her mother Dora tried sell the boy but she felt guilty and rescued him. Dora decided to help the boy and take a trip with him trying to find the boy's father in the Northeast of Brazil. In this trip the boy and the old woman face difficult situations but learned about friendship together. This movies directed by Walter Salles won a Golden Globe in 1998. This movie adapted by Marcos Bernstein and Joao Emanoel Carneiro was an enormous sucess in Brasil even though shows the cruelty reality of some parts of Brazil.
