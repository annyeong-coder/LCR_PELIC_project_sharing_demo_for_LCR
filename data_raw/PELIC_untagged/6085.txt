Frida Kaloo was a famous Mexican artist. She was born in 1907. When she was a little girl she had polio. For this reason she couldn't walk. When she was 12 she could walk again. When she was 18 she had another accident. She couldn't walk for a second time but she could paint. After she met her husband Diego Rivera she could walk again. Eventhough times in her life she couldn't walk she became a famous painter.
