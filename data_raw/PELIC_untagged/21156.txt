Two Pyramid Food Guide created by United States Department of Agriculture USDA which make up healthy diet. The Food Guide Pyramid in the book devides the foods into six groups the Grain Group Vegetable Group Fruit Group Milk Group Meat Group and Fats Group. However the new Pyramid Food Guide categorized foods into seven parts Grains Vegetables Fruits Oils Meat and Beans Milk and Pysical Activity. In both sides shows Grains provides energy for body Vegetables and fruits supply vitamins and minerals Milk and Meat offer calcium and protein but only need a little Oils gives fat it's better to eat a small quantity each day. In addition New food guide advocate to do exercise every day to keep healthy. In general food guide gived directions of daily diet.
