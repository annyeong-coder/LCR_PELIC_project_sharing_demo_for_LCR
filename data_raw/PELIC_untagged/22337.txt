The environmental contamination caused by pollution is a big problem in Guatemala City. A recent research revealed that Guatemala City is the capital more contaminated en Central America. This is caused by emission of gases of more than 4 000 cars that circulate every day. But pollution is also tangible in the country side. The famous lake Atitlan for example is one of many natural sources victims of pollution. Nevertheless pollution is not only caused by the cars and the industry the noise pollution is other environmental problem in the city. Another research assures that Guatemala City is also the noisiest city in the region. Noise levels the research said are higher than those recommended by The World Health Organization.
