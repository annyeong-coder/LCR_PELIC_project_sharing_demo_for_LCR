This is a story about normal people who have a special ability synesthesia. These people are no on drugs and they don't have any brain disease. These people are born with a synesthesia. However they sometimes feel they are isolated because they feel and see objects in different ways than normal people. Truth is there are other people who have synesthesia near them especially families and relatives. Scientist found that these people's brain is responding different way than normal people like when they hear sound within brain audio part and visual part are responding together. These days scientists know that synesthesia exists but they still don't know what causes it. Despite of these research people who have synesthesia want the world to be flat.
