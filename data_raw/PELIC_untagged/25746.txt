The Power of Coincidence A coincidence is a phenomenon that has interests many people whether they believe in it or not. There is a disagreement between the skeptics and the believers about the concept of coincidence. Skeptics believe that a coincidence does not mean anything and it is a group of events that are affected by the law of probabilities. On the other hand believers think that coincidence is the intentional incidence of two things that do not seem to be connected to each other but they are. Many studies tried to explain this phenomenon by using numbers statistics and probabilities or by doing experiments and projects.
