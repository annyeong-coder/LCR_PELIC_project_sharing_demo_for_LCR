It is easy to make gum flowers for decoration for the party. If you want to learn you have to buy one paper celefone one green paper 20 bamboo stick the size the 20 inches one packet of gum and one glue. Then follow the procedures. First cut 15 cm the celefone square. Second put the gum in the middle of the celefone. Next get one stick and put it in the middle of the gum. Then put colored gum around the principal gum. And wrap the gum together with the stick and them glue. Finally for to appear as the shaft of a flower you have to glue the stick and wrap them with green crepe and your flower is ready. You can make many gum flowers and put them in a little jar or offer them to your guests. This procedure is easy and your party going to be amazing.
