Internet effects on businesses The Internet is the biggest invention in last few decades. It has made a huge difference in the human life and has affected all of our life aspects. On of these aspects that has directly influenced is the business. The Internet has some positive effects on businesses such as business globalization and price decreasing. And also some negative effects like missing the personal meeting. Just after the birth of this great technology and as a consequence of that the global became like one market. And this has helped so many companies to find new markets to sell their products in.
