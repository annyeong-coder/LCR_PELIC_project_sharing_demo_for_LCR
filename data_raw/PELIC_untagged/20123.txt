Studying of the ELI is very beneficial Studying at the ELI is very beneficial for students who study foreign language. The first benefit is repetition. First of all foreign language is important to repeat. Learning English takes time. Repetition makes perfect. The second is discussion about how to solve the problem with students. Discussion of the subject is very helpful to students. Through the discussions students can understand the teacher's explanation well. The third is the test or review. Before the test students need to review what they have learned. Thus test is very effective to students for review. After test students can improve their studying ability. In conclusion it is very beneficial to study English in the ELI which educate the students through repetition discussion and test or review.
