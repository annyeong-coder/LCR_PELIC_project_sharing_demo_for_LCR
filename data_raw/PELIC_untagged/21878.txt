The traditional wedding in Congo is the most important moment for the family. First the man must send an require letter to the family of his future wife. About few days after the give an acceptant letter then the both of family decide together about the date and set the time. On the days before celebration the tow families prepare themselves for all things. On the day of wedding woman do all she have to do in the morning after that she and her family wait for the housband's family. About 2 00 in the afternoon the ceremony can begin. First the tow representations of each family speak and give advice to the married then they agree for the wedding. Soon the party start and the people bring their gifts. Later they dring and eat and dance and smile. Finaly the housband take his wife and bring her to his home.
