Andrea Bocelli She was born in small farm in Italy. She started learning piano when she was 6 years old. When she turned 6 she met with an accident. As a result of this an accident she wasn't able to see anything. But she couldn't give up her cheerful life so she made an export. Therefore she could graduate University of Passat and became a lawyer. But because of the passion of music she took a vocal music lesson after she retired her job. Finally she became a great musician who was able to sing very well. She made a new genre of opera popera.
