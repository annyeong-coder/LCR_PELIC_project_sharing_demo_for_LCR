Colorado authorities announced that the balloon boy story was a hoax. The whole story was fabricated by the balloon boy's parents. Authorities found out that the Heenes the boy's family had underlying motivations. They wanted to make a publicity stunt to hopefully they could participate in a reality TV show. The truth emerged for the first time when the boy said in Larry King Show that he thought the event was done for a show . The sheriff said this was the first aha moment which triggered the authorities doubts about the incredibility of the balloon story. The parents did not acknowledge that they organized a hoax. In spite of their denial the Heenes are expected to face charges of conspiracy and making a false report.
