The underlying cause for many people to live in a big town is the facilities which are available such as diversity of transportation. Moreover you can enjoy quietness and safty in rural city. If you want to socialize with other people small city is the best place for that comparing to urban cities where most of the people are busy. There are many ways for intertainment in urban cities like malls cinema zoo and restaurants but most of which not available in small ctities.
