When the prospecter mired down in the musker boggy country to find gold he ran into danger. Although he tried to find a secure place for sleeping he didn't find and he just went to sleep. When he met the warrior in his dream the warrior was about to kill him if he didn't go outside right now but he wanted to know how to escape here on condition of going out. After he woke up he met a woman who has a glowing feature and then she changed a rabbit. She guided them in order to go out so he escaped from dangerous place.
