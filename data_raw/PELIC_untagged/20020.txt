In contrast to snout unlike Crocodiles' snout which is narrower and V shaped Alligators' snout is broader and U shaped. Even though they have a different snout They have a same family of animals called Reptiles or crocodilians. Contrary to teeth Alligators' teeth stick out of jaw whereas crocodiles don't. Moreover although Alligators' teeth are visible Crocodiles' teeth are not visible when their teeth are closed. However both have a same body shape such as long bodies tails and short legs. Unlike Crocodiles Alligators are usually gray or black and darker than Crocodiles which are usually olive or tan colored. Although they have different skin color they have a same diet. They are called Carnivores.
