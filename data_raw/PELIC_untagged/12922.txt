The high cost of gasoline is a problem that makes people have annoyed from last year. At first this problem had been regarded as a small problem but now people start to realize that this problem affects many social and economical factors and have potential power to change the value of almost everything around the world. There are three main effects of the high cost of gasoline. First the transportation system cannot work well. As you know almost all the transportation vehicles use gasoline. Second all the prices of items start to rising. One reason of this rising is because some items are made from oil. The other reason is because the cost of transfer or utilities such as electricity and gas are rising. Third the crimes related with gasoline have been increasing. The numbers of robbing gasoline from the vehicles are obviously rising. This problem might have the potential to make the world unstable. In conclusion there are three effects of the high cost of gasoline that we should solve.
