Foreign students should be allowed to work in the country they are studying. There are a lot of students who have a financial problem. There are several arguments about allowing to work in foreign country giving satisfaction for students development of cross cultural communication. First if students is allowed to work they can leave from financial problem and they can satisfy his life and concentrate their study. Being satisfied is a crucial issue that they can study a lot and get a lot skill. Then they can bring back their high skill to their country. Second if students work at the local they can develop of cross cultural communication through works. Communication with local makes people learn diverse culture. Also students gain good emotion through the communication. As the result students carry their high skills and good emotion across the country. In the future students can contribute for globalization. Students should allowed to work in the country they study for satisfaction and development of cross culture.
