How to prepare a lintel soup Ingredients 1 onion 1tomato 1carrot 1celery 1garlic 1potato 1cup of red lintel 2cups of water 1table spoon of olive oil 1tea spoon of salt Black pepper. Direction Chip the onion put it with the olive oil in a pan use medium heat at this time Slice the potato celery and the tomato then add it to the onion after 5minutes Add the lintel and pour the water let boil together for 2minute then decrease Decrease the heat to the low level let boil for 15mintes then blend the ingred ients in a blender serve it with garlic bread if you like.
