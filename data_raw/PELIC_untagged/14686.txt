This article is talking about the research helping countries to identify what industries should be focus on in order to spur economic growth. Unlike other researches based on label or skill level the main study in this writing is focus on the acceptable goods can be easily branch into. The whole study was based on the global export data research created by Hausmann and Klinger which looks like a complex network with spots and lines. They use nodes as different kinds of goods and lines as their connection between each nodes. According to the study they find the products from the same category may not be appropriate to investigate as their export industry because different types of goods may have better connection between the main export goods.
