The first day of the Chinese New Year is the welcoming of the gods of the heavens and earth. Many people abstain from meat on the first day of the new year because it is believed that this will ensure long and happy lives for them. With the arrival of New Year's Day life is renewed. People will go to the temple and pray to Buddha wishing to have a good and success year. This is the most important event on New Year's Day. People will wear new clothes today. Visits are made to friends neighbors and relatives to exchange good wishes of gong xi fa cai which means congratulations and prosperity. Together .
