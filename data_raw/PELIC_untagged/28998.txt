A Sixth Sense is an article about an ordinary people with extraordinary sense or scientifically called synesthesia. This article describes synesthesia as a rare brain condition in which senses hooked together. It means people with synesthesia has a joined sensation. This article talks about people who see and feel things that other doesn't. This article reveal their experience and feeling in which tells how this innate ability makes they feel isolated from other. Besides that this article also talks about medical explanation of synesthesia and what is happening inside the synestheses's brain. Author makes an interesting conclusion by stated different views and causes of synesthesia which will indirectly help reader understand the factors contribute to this problem.
