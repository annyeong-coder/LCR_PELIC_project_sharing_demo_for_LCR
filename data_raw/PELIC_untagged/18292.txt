Number of students at Springfield Academy The line graph of Springfield Academy displayed the population of students. From 1992 to 1995 the number of foreign students remains the same but in this period of time the number of American students increased slowly. In 1996 the number of foreign students rose to equal the number of American students. Between 1996 and 1998 the number of American students decreased. From 1996 to 2002 the number of foreign students was more than the number of American students. Between 1999 and 2001 the number of American students increased again. The number of American students fell again in 2001. So in general the total number of students between 1992 and 2002 increased.
