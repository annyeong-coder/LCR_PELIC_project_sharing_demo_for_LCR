How to make a cake Do you like sweet If you like a cake this recipe would be your favorite. It is not difficult to make a cake if you follow these steps. First you have to prepare some ingredients. They are four eggs one cup of sugar half cup of flour and one teaspoon of baking powder. Second you whisk the eggs and sugar for ten minutes or until they are fluffy. Third you add flour and baking powder and beat together until they are mixed well. Fourth you pour them into the baking pan. Finally you bake them for ten minute in the oven heated 375 F. You can eat them with whipping cream fruit and so on. As you can see making a cake is easy. Additionally all ingredients are not special. When you want to eat sweet you can make a delicious cake soon if you use this recipe.
