The author introduces his ideas by a short story about a miniature horse called panda. He talks in this article about the advantages and disadvantages of using miniature horses as service animals and what people think about that. Horses are mild mannared easy to be trained and have a great range of vision moreover they live longer than dogs. Because horses live longer than dogs the chance to deeper communication with people is bigger. Also they are less agressive than dogs. On the other hand horses eat and go to the bathroom more frequently than dogs. Also they need a big space to fit in due to their big size. The U. S. Government may control the places in which service animals are used. Due to their potential effects on people health safety and comfort some rules should be set up to balance the rights and needs of people who use these animals.
