Shady's team found 32 flutes made of the bones seabirds and they uncovered 37 cornets made of animal. They prove that music played an important role in Caral's society. Caral farmers grew the cotton that fishermen needed to make the net. Shady discovered a hierarchy in living arrangements. Rich and elite people live large well kept rooms craftsmen live ground level and workers live older and poorer homes far from the city center.
