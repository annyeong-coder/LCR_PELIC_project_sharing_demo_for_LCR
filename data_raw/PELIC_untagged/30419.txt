This novel is about Cassie's mother participation with her daughter in a TV reality show called Lost and Found. This show is about a game where there is a number of teams traveling around the world searching for various items and collecting them. In this novel Cassie's mother is talking about her relation with her daughter which has problems. And she mentioned some of the events that happened during the game. At the end she wrote the main motive of her participation in this show which is to get closer to her daughter and to solve the problems between them.
