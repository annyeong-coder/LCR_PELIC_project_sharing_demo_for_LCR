In A chimp Off the Old Block Discover magazine January 2002 the author reports Ayumu at Kyoto University's Primate Research Institute has mimicked his mother's behavior. His mother Ai has been taught several dozen kanji characters. Ai has observed that she matches colors with their kanji and uses coins she receives for doing her task to buy snacks. On February he performed to get coins as she did. Researchers were surprised the fact that Ai educated by himself how to read and use coins.
