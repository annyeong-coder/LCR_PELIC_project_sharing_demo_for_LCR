Big Bang Theory Valentine's day Episode In this episode Leonard receives an invitation for him and a guest to attend a conference of the Super Collider in Switzerland. He invites his girlfriend Penny but his best friend Sheldon gets upset and try to persuade first Leonard and the Penny to let him go. At the end Penny and Sheldon get sick so Leonard had to travel with Raj who had been mocked all week long for not having a Valentine 's day plan.
