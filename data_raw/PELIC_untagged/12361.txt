The six American values in education 1. There is no payment in public education. 2. Any citizen has the same right to have education regarding their race religion or social status. 3. The public schools are not including any religious beliefs in their activities. 4. The state and local governments have power or authority to regulate the public schools in their territories. 5. It is required by the law to attend school usually between the ages of 7 and 16. 6. The public schools should be improved by adding activities that provide young people to grow their potential.
