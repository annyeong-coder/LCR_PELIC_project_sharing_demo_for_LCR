The Effects of the Internet Business In these times when Internet seems to make anything possible and when the information is exploded anywhere on line it is demonstrated the revolution of the businesses among corporations and marketing. The article Strategy and the Internet that Michael Porter wrote in the Harvard Business Review in March 2001. The lesson from the article is that we should not lose the focus on strategic development and competitive advantage. It was true then and it is true now. According to Porter there are three relevant effects of the Internet business strategy that is very benefit in making information widely available reducing the difficulty of purchasing marketing and distribution and allowing buyers and sellers to find and transact business with one another more easily.
