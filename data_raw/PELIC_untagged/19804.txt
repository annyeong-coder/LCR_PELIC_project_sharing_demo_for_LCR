Food is one of the four basic necessities in our lives. Most people give the first priority to food. Some people say that we eat in order to live but some say we live in order to eat. From my experience I have tried many national foods. I also have chance to try American food. In my opinion American food is totally different from my Thai food. The purpose of this essay is to compare and contrast the differences between American food and Thai food. The three main differences are food portion cost and flavor.
