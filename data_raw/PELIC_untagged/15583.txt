Stress at work can be a serious problem. As you know we usually have stress when we work so hard because we have to solve and deal with some important plans. Stress makes us always feel tired and we can't pay attention for our jobs. So that effect of stress is very seriously. There are several ways to reduce stress. First of all we should take a little time to relax. For example when we feel so tense in mind we should take a nap take a shower. After that we listen to music or watch movies which helps us a lot to feel comfortable because we can stop thinking about working. Another way we should joint a sport even or work out that is healthy. If we are healthy we will work better.
