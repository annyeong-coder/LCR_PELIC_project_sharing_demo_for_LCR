Sawiris is a great director and actress who make a film to try to change the image of Arabs in USA. This film was make in jordan the place who her wants to show to the world. She worked initially with a famous director where she learn a lot about her career and then she hires actors and actress from Arabs and American to improve the cality of her film and to introduce it at international comunity film. The theme of this film was her semi autobiographic epic love story of denial the search for and ultimately the acceptance of self .
