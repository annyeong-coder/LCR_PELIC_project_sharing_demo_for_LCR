Culture expectations of success There are different culture expectations of success for males and females. Success for men usually means powerful a successful career and success for women usually means taking care of children. In the modern society a career and power are taking as the mark of a successful man. Many higher positions of the government and a lot of international company CEOs are men. It is the result of our culture expectation. On the other hand a successful woman usually means that she take care of children in good condition and usually manages a home successfully. If a child grows up with some achievements we usually say that the mother has succeeded. However there are still a few successful women holds a higher position in the government and a few women are CEOs.
