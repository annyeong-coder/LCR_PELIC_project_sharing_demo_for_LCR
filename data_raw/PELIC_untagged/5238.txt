Topic This is some advice to a new ELI student to help them succeed in learning English. This is some advice to a new ELI student to help them succeed in learning English. The most important thing is if you get any opportunities you must talk with native speaker and promote your speaking skill. Second in your free time you can watch television and movies with captions because that is importance to your listening. Third you must go to the class and not skip classes. Fourth you also have to study a lot. Finally if you have any question you must ask the question and not be shy in the class.
