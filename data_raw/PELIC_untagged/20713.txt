In the age of computers finding a book in a library has become very easy. There are two catalogs one for books and one for periodicals magazines newspapers and so on when you find the computers that contain the library's catalogs. Before scroll through the entries for the books that are displayed on the computer screen and determine which ones seem the most relevant you type the topic you are seeking information about in the space labeled subject when you seem the catalogs. Afte that you write down the title and call number of each book that you want as soon as locate the books on the library shelves by their call numbers. After you find it you should be prepared to show your library card at the checkout desk. Whenever you show your library card you take the books to the checkout desk.
