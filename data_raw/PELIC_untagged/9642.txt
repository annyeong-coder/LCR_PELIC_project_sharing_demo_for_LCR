It is simple to make a bookcase if you follow these steps. First of all you should prepare some materials such as wood nails glue a hammer a saw sandpaper and paint. Then cutting the wood to two side pieces one back piece two molding strips and three pieces for top bottom and shelf. Next you can utilize the sandpaper to sand each piece of wood. In addition you have to glue one molding strip to each piece. Furthermore nailing side pieces to bottom. Besides you need to nail the top and the back in place. Moreover you can paint the bookcase and shelf in the colors that you anticipate. Most importantly let the bookcase dry for two hours. It is a crucial step because it might trigger the paint attach to your books if the bookcase is still wet. Finally you can slide the shelf into place. These steps are not rigid you also can modify some decorations on the bookcase by yourself.
