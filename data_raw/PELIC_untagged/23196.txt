The happy time always short an Arabic saying which describes the fun time when it replaced by unexcited one. Pre school period is the most effective time to boom the child's energy and creativity as well as shaping his characteristic. Unfortunately this situation will not be existed in the formal and regular school period. Obviously in kindergarten the little students are allowed to talk read listen write draw play giggle and cry. All these actions are aspects of feeling expression. However in the school period listening to the teachers doing lot homework and passing the final exams are the permanent activities. As a consequence of that the students are going to lose their sense of enjoyment of initiating non academic creativity because the last is encircled and suppressed this form of education and teaching.
