People think that coaches and business men are different jobs because of working place and kinds of work but they have similarities for some reasons. First coaches and business men who are responsible for training their athletes or employees are the task leading the team members or employees. Also their abilities are to solve problems between teammates and employees that can make work place running softly. In addition they must stand for their subordinate to the management of higher management. Moreover they perform middlemen between players and owners or lower level employees and CEOs.
