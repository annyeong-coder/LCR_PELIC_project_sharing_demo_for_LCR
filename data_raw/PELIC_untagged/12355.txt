There are the 6 traditional values underlying the U. S. Educational system. 1. Public education should be not cost. Any citizen can receive a good education. They dont need to pay anything. 2. Schooling cant treat any one unfair because of prejudice. Anyone has the same rights and chances for education. 3. The publish schools cant restrict peoples belief and faith. No special prayer or Bible reading is required. But religious schools are allowed outside of the publish school system. 4. The state and local governments control Public schools. 5. Attendance at school is necessary. Usually between the ages of 7 and 16 young people are forced attend at school each state. 6. Schooling doesnt only do basic things. Schools are required to improve the quality of education.
