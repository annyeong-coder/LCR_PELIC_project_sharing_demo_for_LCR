Miss Hannah has been living alone in her house which located nearly shore and far away from other people houses. She has brother named Ralph who is run away from home for many years. She still hopes that he will come back and will be a rich man. One day while she was doing job in her garden Jacob and his niece come to visit her. He asks her to give him some flower in order to decorate his house for party. Jacob tells Miss Hannah that he think Ralph won't come back. That make Miss Hannah feel angry. One day when Ralph came back Miss Hannah was very happy. She realizes that even though Ralph doesn't be rich she still feel happy about to living with him.
