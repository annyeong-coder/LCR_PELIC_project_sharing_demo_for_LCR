Peak NOUN def. The point at which a process or an activity is at its strongest most successful or most fully developed. Cf. The party's membership has fallen from a peak of fifty thousand after the Second World War. Basis NOUN def. If something is done on a particular basis it is done according to that method system or principle. Cf. We're going to be meeting there on a regular basis. Monitor VERB def. If you monitor something you regularly check its development or progress and sometimes comment on it. Cf. Officials had not been allowed to monitor the voting. Engage VERB def. If you engage in an activity you do it or are actively involved with it. Cf. I have never engaged in the drug trade. Adept ADJ def. Someone who is adept at something can do it skilfully. Cf. He's usually very adept at keeping his private life out of the media. Key NOUN def. A key is a specially shaped piece of metal that you place in a lock and turn in order to open or lock a door or to start or stop the engine of a vehicle. Cf. They put the key in the door and entered. Ritual ADJ def. A ritual is a religious service or other ceremony which involves a series of actions performed in a fixed order. Cf. This is the most ancient and holiest of the Shinto rituals.
