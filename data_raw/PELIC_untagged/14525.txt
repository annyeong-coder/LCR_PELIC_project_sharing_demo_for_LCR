Eating fruit and vegetables on a daily basis is essential to maintain us healthy. Fruits and vegetables give us many of the nutrients that we need vitamins minerals dietary fiber folate and potassium. All of these useful nutrients may protect you from getting chronic diseases. Compared with people who consume a diet with only small amounts of fruits and vegetables those who eat more amounts as part of a healthful diet are likely to have reduced risk of chronic diseases including stroke and perhaps other cardiovascular diseases and some types of cancer. Fruit has many different forms to eat fresh frozen canned dried and juice. All are good ways to boost your fruit intake. Like Fruit vegetables are available not only fresh but also frozen canned. Also we can eat them raw steamed boiled stir fried or baked. These ways make vegetables more flavorful to help us eat them.
