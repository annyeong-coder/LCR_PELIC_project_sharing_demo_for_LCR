Adrian who is a geologist gets a warning from his friend. His friend believes that the Earth will get extremely disordered according to some evidences such as a reaction of neutrino and the increasing temperature in inner earth. Therefore Adrian reports this severe situation to an official and the president of the U. S. And then a plan starts to be implemented secretly. In addition Jackson who is a writer takes his two kids to camp on a mountain and finds this secret accidently through a guy named Charlie. Charlie reveals lots of information about spaceship earth crust displacement and a map to find ship and that triggers Jackson's a serious of actions to save his family and look for the ship which can help them to survive.
