The production and sale of cigarettes should not be made illegal. Everyone knows that smoking is harmful to the health. Definitely smokers should break their habit of smoking. However making the production and sale of cigarettes illegal is a different issue. Today there are millions of smokers in the world. If the production and sale of cigarettes were made illegal what would happen It is very difficult for smokers to give up smoking and they will desperately try to get cigarettes. We can easily imagine that some companies start producing and selling cigarettes secretly and illegally. Some people especially young ones may have more interest in something that is illegal and it could cause confusion to the society. It is no use just prohibiting the production and sale of cigarettes.
