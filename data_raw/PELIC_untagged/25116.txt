Children's Festival Children Festival is very relevant to Turkish Republic. It is a gift from our first president after the I. World War. It was given them in a short time after The Turkish Republic established. It is celebrated on 23rd of April Children celebrate it with different activities. There are a few steps that children should to demonstrate at this day. First they go their school for the beginning ceremony. After the beginning ceremony they go the biggest stadium for to continue the celebration. Finally they demonstrate their show which they previously have prepared for this day. Beginning ceremony stars very early in the morning. Students come their school and wait for everyone even who doesn't have a role in show group must join this ceremony. When all students come they go in front of the statue of our first president and repeat our National March. Then teachers tell them about concept of the ceremony and steps. After that they will be ready for to move the biggest show stadium. The biggest stadium usually is be ready for this important celebrations. When all schools come mayor of the city stars to talk about our history and ceremony continues with a few teachers and students supplies. They encourage students before their show because after they finish their supplies students will demonstrate their performance. Finally students are ready for their show. Approximately before a month they start to prepare their show. First teachers decide to create groups. Then trainers help them for preparations. Every group perform different talents. They usually wear colorful costumes and the stadium looks very different from the beginning. To summary Children Festival is really important and memorable day for children may be they have to do some rituals they have to come school early they have to go stadium and they show their talents but at the and of the celebration they feel free and inordinately proud.
