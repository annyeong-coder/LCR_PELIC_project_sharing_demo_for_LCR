A survey published Sunday said that the price of gallon of gasoline had jumped back up near 3 a gallon. The reporter said that the Lundberg Survey found the average price for a gallon of self serve regular had been 2. 96 On Friday up 16cents from the previous survey two weeks earlier. Publisher Trillby Lundberg said that if crude oil prices didn't retreat gas prices could easily cross back over the 3 mark on average. The reporter said that prices had reached an all time high of 3. 18 In May. Lundberg said that the current price 2. 96 Was 78 cents higher that it had been one year before. The reporter said that the survey looked at thousands of gas stations across the country it had found the highest average price for a gallon of self serve regular had been in San Francisco at 3. 28 And the lowest had been in Newark New Jersey at 2. 73.
