Before you come to study in the USA you have to prepare. You should get enough information about this country so that your life here does not become difficult. You ought to read websites or to ask people who were already in the USA about food weather custom conventions life style. You should be ready to speak English. Even if you're going to study English you'd better prepare some necessary phrases that you'll need to use at the airport on a bus in a store or a caf I ou don't understand something or if you need help ask somebody. Usually American people are very friendly and they will help you.
