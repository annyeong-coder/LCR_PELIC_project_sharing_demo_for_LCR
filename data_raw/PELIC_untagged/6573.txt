Topic types of mistakes people make when learning a second language free writing and brainstorming Learning a second language differs from one to another. However there are common mistakes people make in their learning a second language. We need to be aware about these mistakes to improve our learning. Mistakes of learning second language can be divided into three types mistakes in learning style mistakes in managing time and mistakes in monitoring. Mistakes in learning style focus in grammar rules with out noticing them in listening and speaking. Choose one way to learn instead using multi choices. Less practicing. Mistakes in managing time to do home work when we are tired. To Give unbalance time for each different language skills. To not use more time in the parts we have a lake of ability in. Mistakes in monitoring To wait others to correct our mistakes. To not realize the position of the mistakes. To try use word just from their meaning not how it use in the contest
