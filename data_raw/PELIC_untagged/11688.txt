Banality of evil implies that how normal people were engaged in such an experiment in which they obeyed all irrational commends of the authority. Milgram's study demonstrated how hard subjects try physically and psychologically to be obedient and therefore successful individuals on their given tasks. It shows that people can act cruelly under specific circumstance such as when they are under pressure. Milgrams' theory proves that there is an evil presenting in everybody's personalities. The evil side of some people is revealed at particular conditions.
