Do you know a reform The reform is to make a new things that utilize a old clothes. The reform is very easy. First prepare the old clothes or not wear any longer clothes. Second think about your to make things. Next draw a design on your clothes. Then cut and sew it. Here is a tip about cutting. You have allowance from cut line to 2 inch when you cut. Finally try to use the your a product. You will like the this because this is your handmade product.
