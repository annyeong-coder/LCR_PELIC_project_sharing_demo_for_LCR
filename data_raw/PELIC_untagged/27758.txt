The article talks about study of how love can relief pain and work as morphine or painkiller in the human brain. The study include 15 people in the age of 19 to 21 the participant asked them to bring pictures for their lover and attractive people who know. Then they asked them distraction questions while the computer give them pain on their palm. The FMRI scanner functional magnetic resonance imaging shows that love has the same effect of morphine in the brain and the thinking of lover lower the perception of pain. Also the study show that love has the same function of cocaine in the brain. On the other hand people who have divorce or breakup their relationship suffer more pain than other.
