Learning English is essential for people from non English speaking countries to live in the global world. The first beginning when English has been widespread is the United Kingdom's colonies from the 17th century to the 19th century. After World War II the United States has reigned over the world as a super power country. As a result English has been spoken in conferences and meetings between different countries. It has been also written in documents which are important to contract treaties or trades. In the present world if people from non English speaking countries don't know English they will definitely have disadvantages to do international business with other countries. Also it will be hard to be adept at living the world which is getting to be globalization. So it's important for people from non English speaking countries to learn English
