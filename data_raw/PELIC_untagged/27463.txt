In this era with advanced technology many kinds of pollution have been increasing such as air pollution and water pollution. Air pollution could be caused by two reasons first is natural reason like volcanoes which we can't control. The second is caused by many factors like fuel burning from cars and factories or various agriculture activities such as using of pesticides and chemical fertilizers. Air pollution triggers many human diseases such as asthma and shortness of breath. Moreover it affects buildings animals and agriculture crops. There are many solutions can be done like improving energy efficiency in transport and industry.
