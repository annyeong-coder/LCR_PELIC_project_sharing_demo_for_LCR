This article is about how the United States is a country of immigrants. After America was discovered the Europeans began to immigrate. Although those American citizens are immigrants they look down to the new arrivals. The new arrivals try to adopt the American culture fast. They usually took jobs that established Americans do not want. For example they took jobs that involve hard physical work or are dangerous. Moreover Most immigrants come to the US for economic reasons to have better life. Today America is considered a mosaic with immigrants adding their cultural heritage to the mix of American culture.
