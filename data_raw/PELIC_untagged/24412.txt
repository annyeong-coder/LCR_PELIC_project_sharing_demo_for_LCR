Sao Paulo has an enormous problem with flood every year. During the summer this year the news reported that storms had caused 18 deaths in Sao Paulo. They said that 158 cities of Sao Paulo had suffered because of storms. They told that 21 918 people had to leave their houses and stayed in a shelter. The population told that they needed support from the government and they needed help to go back to a normal life. The population wondered if the government would help or not. They added that the government should be prepared for these emergency situations and they should invest money and some work to prevent flood in the city of Sao Paulo. The population said that they had to face this kind of problem every year and the government didn't do anything to change this situation. Finally the government responded that they would invest 50 million in areas with imminent risk of flood.
