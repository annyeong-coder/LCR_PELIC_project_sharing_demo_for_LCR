Every community has some myths although it is thought that myth is not true but the myth is repeated for along time. My community is one of these communities has a lot of myths that I have heard since I was child. It is alleged that some fairies talk to people and sometime are human's form. It is said that a person was married to a fairy her conditions was that he did not say mashallah I f this ward is heard the fairy will hide. They had three children and she worked with him she cooked and serviced for him. That day it is thought that he forgot and said this ward . Therefore she hid and took one of her children but his father caught half of his body before he disappeared. According to this myth there are some people mixed from human and fairies. It is alleged that some people saw this kind of families and some of them got married to people. They are thought to live among us but we do not see them.
