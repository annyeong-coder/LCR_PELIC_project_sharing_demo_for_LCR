The great achievements now available in the realm of communication are playing a major role in our everyday life. Obvious examples of efficient communication devices are the mobile phone and the internet. In addition to the traditional way of using a mobile phone mobile phone users can send text messages voice messages and make video calls. Likewise the internet is providing new features every now and then. From emailing and making video calls to sharing photos and videos with friends from all over the globe. In short the mobile phone and the internet are the ultimate means of communication of our present time.
