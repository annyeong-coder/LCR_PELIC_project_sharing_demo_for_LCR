Learning New Language Learning new language is a complex process. First of all you should initially acquire the essential skills. For example you have to learn the vocabulary of the new language. Furthermore you should learn how to write and pronounce the words. Second the learning process can take long time and that depend on different aspects. For example my target date to finish my classes in English is in May. Which means I need one year to learn this new language. Finally your effort is obviously relevant to how quickly you learn the new language. The more work you do the bigger success you achieve. In conclusion Learning new language requires a lot of work and time but your effort still has the greatest role in your learning process.
