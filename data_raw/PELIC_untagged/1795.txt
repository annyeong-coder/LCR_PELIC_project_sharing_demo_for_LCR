Korea my county is located in Eastern southern Asia. Originally Korea was only one nation about 50 years ago but it has been divided into 2 countries because of the difference of the political opinions. Korean is used as a native language. Rice is mainly grown in Korea especially in western southern area. Koreans have many kinds of pets but the dog is mainly considered as a pet. In rural area of Korea cows and pigs are usually raised as the edible meat products.
