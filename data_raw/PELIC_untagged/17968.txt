My Friend With His Disability My friend his name is Esam. In 1999 he lost one of his legs by an accident. Even he couldn't walk normaly he was able to continue his study and he works at the Iron Steel Company in my country. He can use the computer and the internet very well. Alos Esam has a car and he can drive it well He drives his car every day to go to his work. By the way three years ago Esam got marry. Now he has a son he is very happy and he lives as a normal person.
