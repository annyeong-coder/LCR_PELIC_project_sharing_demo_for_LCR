Online Learning VS. Traditional Classroom Learning Both online learning and traditional class learning have some differences as well as similarities. In fact they differ in having discipline arranging schedule and size of class while they have almost the same content. Unlike traditional learning online learning has lack of discipline. Bothe online learning and traditional learning have the same content. In online learning schedule is arranged depending on the free time of the students whereas in traditional learning it is determined by the administration at the beginning of year. Student studies alone as an individual in online learning in contrast he or she can study with a group of students in traditional school.
