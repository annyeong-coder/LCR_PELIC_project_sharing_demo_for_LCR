The documentary is talking about Virginia Woolf the English novelist and writer. Virginia was born in 1882. It describes her life and how she raised up in an enlightened family where the father was a writer and most of their visitors were literary figures. Although that Virginia received her education at home while her brothers went to universities and school. Since Virginia lost many members of her family while she was still young she had many breakdowns during her life. As a result of her difficult life Virginia found a shelter in her writings. She wrote many good novels short stories and essays. She became a figure in English literature. She married Leonard Woolf. Finally she committed suicide and died in 1941
