A new international conference will begin on Sunday July 17 2010. A new plan announced by Obama administration aims to reduce HIV infections by 25 within 5 years. HIV is a virus causing AIDS. There are one million HIV infections in the U. S and 33 million infections in the worldwide. 65 Americans infected get treatment within 3 months. The new plan calls for increasing that to 85 . Also government scientists announced that two antibodies were discovered and there is a hope for an AIDS vaccine. The two antibodies were found in a man who produced them naturally. The two antibodies can stop 90 strains of HIV.
