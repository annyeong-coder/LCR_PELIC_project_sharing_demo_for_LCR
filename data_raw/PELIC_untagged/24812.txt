Why Male Children are Preferred More Than Female Children Sometimes parents prefer to have male children rather than female children. Although values are depend on each people and not always true many parents want to have male children. There are two typical reason of this preference Parents want to live with their children and want to continue their families. First case is that parents want to live with their children after children get older. Until recently it is a usual thing that three generations grandparents parents and children live together. Most common case is that oldest son continues to live together after he gets older. By living together people can share earning knowledge and help when someone needs care. However if parents want to live with their children female children are more unreliable than male children. Because women generally leave their parents house after merry in many case parents can' t live together with their female children. By this reason parents who want to live with their children prefer to have male children who are more reliable. Second reason is to continue their family. In some case Japanese people think family history as important thing and it is considered that only male children can take over their family. For example family name is usually take over for male children because women change their family name when they marry. In addition if a family own their business it is preferred to the oldest son take over family business. In summary Japanese parents prefer to have male children when they want to continue their family.
