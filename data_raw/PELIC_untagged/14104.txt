We can't achieve many targets without using Internet. As example Internet becomes the best way for entertainment education and businesses. Electronic trade or E commercial became very popular in many countries. Therefore the scholars of Business had care of this new evolution. Second Internet created a new business. These days there are global companies around the world offer their services to users everywhere. They make very huge businesses. Third Internet becomes the main information resource in stocks markets specially. That makes Internet the most affected factor in stocks markets. Actually Internet control stocks markets by the information war as they call it now. Finally if you don't know how to use Internet try to learn it as soon as possible. In fact Internet now is part of our life we can't live with out it.
