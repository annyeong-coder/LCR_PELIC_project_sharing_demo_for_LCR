Natural sights in Saudi Arabia Saudi Arabia has many distinctive and varied natural sights such as beaches mountains and deserts. Although it does not have any permanent water streams it has very long beaches. Its longest beaches are on the Red Sea in the west. On the other hand it also has long beaches on the Arabian Gulf Persian Gulf in the east. Moreover it has great mountains in the southwest. These mountains range of over 9000 feet above the sea level. The most famous natural sight in Saudi Arabia is the desert since it is the largest sand desert in the world. It is located in the south and called the Rub Al Khali that means the Empty Quarter.
