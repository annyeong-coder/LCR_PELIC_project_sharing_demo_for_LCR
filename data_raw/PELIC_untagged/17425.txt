October 27 . I have been on the canary is lands for three days now . I will start home when the weather is better . I was so surprised when I piked up my mail today . My family sent birthday present to me . My birthday is the 31st. October 29 . I think the weather is getting worse . I heard the thunder today but there was not much rain. Typhoon and I strarted reading the Brave New World. October 30 . I left the canary island today just Columbus . There is strong wind and plenty of sunshine now. I went 250 miles . October 31 .
