A CNN reporter said the price of a gallon of gasoline had jumped back up near 3 a gallon according to a survey published on the previous Sunday. The reporter said the Lundberg Survey had found the average price for a gallon of self serve regular had been 2. 96 On the previous Friday up 16 cents from the previous survey two weeks before. Trilby Lundberg said gas prices could easily cross back over the 3 mark on average if crude oil prices didn't retreat. The reporter said prices had reached an all time high of 3. 18 In the May. Lundberg said the current price 2. 96 Was 78 cents higher than it had been one year before. The reporter said the survey looked at thousands of gas stations across the country. The reporter added it had found the highest average price for a gallon of self serve regular had been in San Francisco at 3. 28 And the lowest had been in Newark New Jersey at 2. 73.
