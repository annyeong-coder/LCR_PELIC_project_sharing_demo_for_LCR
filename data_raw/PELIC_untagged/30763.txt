Studying the animal s behaviors leads to better understanding and useful using of their talents and good cooperation between them and humans. Crows for example have the ability to make a tool in order to get food and sometimes this tool is the humans. They are very intelligent and willing to be trained and able to share their knowledge with others. In addition to crows geckos and their amazing capacity for climbing is a great example of how humans can learn about animals. The chance is big for cooperation between humans and animals in the future if humans let animals stay alive.
