There are several ways to save oil in our lives. First of all I will try to reduce the time of using my car everyday. Second I can do a regular maintenance of my car so that it remains fuel efficient. Third I can share the car with some co workers. Carpool is a good way to save oil. Next I can take the bus to school. After that I can use public transportation to go everywhere. Finally I can use a small car rather than a big one because a small car can save a lot of oil. Oil is very important to our life so everyone should take efforts to save oil. It will help us for the future.
