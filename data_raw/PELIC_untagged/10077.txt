Running over the reasons for which stereotypes exist McCrae and his team decided to investigate how stereotypes are connected to the real and perceived personalities in roughly 50 countries. The team conducted a series of three surveys by which it could have been possible to extract informations about how people rate their personality first two surveys and how people describe a typical person from their country third survey . In conclusion McCrae and his colleagues investigated the connection between the real people and the categorization that groups these people under a stereotype.
