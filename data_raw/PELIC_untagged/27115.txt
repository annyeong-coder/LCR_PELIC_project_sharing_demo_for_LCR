Quality of life The human life is held in the community so our life's problems are also social issues. All our life we are trying to get answers to many questions what is sense of life what is happiness why we became in this world I think social issue quality of life shows the general well being and satisfaction of life of each person in the society. The term quality of life includes a lot of different aspects of the human life for instance material well being health and social life. The important part of quality of life is the material well being of a person. Each person wants to get an educational achievement that will affect on his her employment income and even wealth. If you cannot to gain basic human needs such as food water home you cannot be satisfied with your life in general. For example poverty is defined as a low quality of life. Concept of health in the term quality of life includes emotional and mental health recreation and physical well being. Human health directly affects on all things about feeling good . If you are feeling constant stress sadness and anxiety you cannot enjoy your life even when you have enough money. Conversely if you feel emotional satisfaction joy and have a positive outlook you can achieve many your goals. The social life directly relates to quality of life also. Obviously that your family friends coworkers are persons who always help you to solve difficult problems in your life. Your social status plays important role in the community life. A person cannot be happy if he she does not get their cultural and spiritual needs. Thus it is hard to measure human happiness but we can assess general criterion's of human well being. Of course the term quality of life is very broad and complex. It frequently includes many other important aspects of human life such as freedom human rights etc. I think understanding of the term quality of life can help us to find different ways of making us happier.
