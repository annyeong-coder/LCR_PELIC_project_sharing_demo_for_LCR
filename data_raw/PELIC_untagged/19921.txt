The importance of women has grown with the development of the modern society. First of all there are more women in government now then they were several years ago. Also according to Bureau of Labor Statistics in 1950 percentage of women on managerial administrative position was approximately 13. 8 But in 1980 this quantity changed into 26. 1. Besides there are many companies established by women. Finally there are a lot of women lecturers now whereas there was a time when woman had no right to acquire a higher education.
