Eid Alftr Eid Alftr is one of the Muslim's holy days which takes place on the first day after Ramadan according to Islamic calendar. This day is waited by families and children in All Islamic countries. To illustrate in Saudi Arabia Families start preparing for this day for more than one month. Usually most of the families in Saudi Arabia buy new clothes for this day. They also buy chocolate and gifts. For example gifts are usually watches necklaces and rings. On the previous night of Eid Alftr families in Saudi Arabia cooperate to help poor families. To demonstrate they give them charity which is usually rice and money. The target of this assistant of the poor families is to push them for achieving happiness. On the morning of Eid Alftr each individual wears new clothes and goes to the mosque to pray Eid Alftr prayer. After men finish prayer they gather with their neighborhood and start ceremony. Coffee dates chocolate and gifts are included in this ceremony. In addition sometimes they make a meal such as rice lamb and salad. After that all families gather at their parent's homes. The gifts are exchanged. And some chocolate is given to children. In addition the dinner is made by the parent. In the evening of this day each city makes at least one sophisticated gathering including many activities. First the ceremony is started by fireworks. Second the governor welcomes the guests. After the poems are read aloud there is music and dancing. Beverages are offered in this ceremony. As result of many factors Eid Alftr is the really the most important holy day for all Islamic families. In this day they gather meet relevant meet friends and celebrate however they don't forget helping poor families. This is an unusual day in Saudi Arabia.
