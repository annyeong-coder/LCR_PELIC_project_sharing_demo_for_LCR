Let me show you how to cook Castella . The ingredients you need are eggs sugar flour milk honey and oil. First beat egg whites until they form peaks. Second add sugar to them and stir well. After that add yolks flour milk honey and oil to them and stir well in each case too. When it is glossy pour it into a mold. And then bake it in the oven for forty minutes. Finally remove it from the mold put it upside down and let it cool. This cooking method makes you an expert at Castella .
