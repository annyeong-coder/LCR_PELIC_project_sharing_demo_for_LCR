The depreciation of economy attached all of the world. My country was one of the countries which affected by the economic problem. There were three main effect of the economic problem in my country unemployed the quality of life and the development of the country. First as the consequence of the economic problem many people lost their jobs. The poverty became higher. Many people that they had high education changed their majors to have jobs they did not like. Second due to the economic problem the quality of live has changed. People lost the opportunity to get a good vacation. Others they could not have it because they had not enough money. Also some people they have not money to have insurance so the percentage of death arose. Finally as result of the economic problem the country could not develop. The quality of service has diminished. In addition government could not create new jobs. In conclusion the effects of the economic problem were more unemployed poor quality of life and government cannot develop the country.
