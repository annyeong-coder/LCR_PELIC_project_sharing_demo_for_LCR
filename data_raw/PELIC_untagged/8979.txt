CNN reported that the price of a gallon of gasoline had jumped back up near 3 a gallon according to a survey that had been published Sunday. The reporter said that the Lundberg Survey had found the average price for a gallon of self serve regular had been 2. 96 On Friday up 16 cents from the previous survey two weeks earlier. The publisher Trilby Lundberg said If crude oil prices don't retreat gas prices could easily cross back over the 3 mark on average . The reporter also said that the prices had reached an all time high of 3. 18 In May. Lundberg had said that the current price 2. 96 Is 78 cents higher than it had been one year before. The reporter said that the survey looked at thousands of gas stations across the country. It had found the highest average price for a gallon of self serve regular had been in San Francisco at 3. 28. The lowest had been in Newark New Jersey at 2. 73.
