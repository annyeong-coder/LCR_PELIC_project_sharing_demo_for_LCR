FRIDA KAHLO Frida Kahlo was born on July 6 1907 in Coyoacan Mexico. She was the wife of Mexican painter Diego Rivera. Frida Kahlo was able to be painter. She had an accident when she was 18 years old. As a result of the accident she could not walk anymore. Also she couldn't have babies. She had around 35 operations in her back and in her right leg. After the accident she had a special easel made for her so she could paint in bed. She could continue painting until last day of her life. She could paint 143 paintings. Finally she died on July 13 1954.
