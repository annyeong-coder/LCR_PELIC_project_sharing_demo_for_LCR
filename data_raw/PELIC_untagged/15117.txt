This is a special place big ballroom in the Peterhoff Palace. This place now looks like a museum. This palace located in city Saint Petersburgh of Russian Federation. On the top of the ceiling there are very beautiful pictures. The Greek and Roman statues are on the left side of the room. There are gold color and those pictures about girls and boys dancing with angels. This room has so much light because it has six windows. From the windows you can see Finland Gulf.
