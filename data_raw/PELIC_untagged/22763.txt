Kate Fitzgerald a teenager who is diagnosed with cancer from a young age. Her parents depended on her genetically engineered younger sister to help take organs from her body so she can live. Her mother loved her so much that she was very attached to her and would do anything to make her survive. Kate told her younger sister that she should sue her parents in order for them to hate her and give up on her so she can die. Her younger sister did what she was told and the parents got very suspiscious that the girls were up to something. She could not take it anymore and needed some peace from the world. Her parents later on found out of the plan and soon her mother understood where she came from and knew her daughter was very sick and just wanted to die. It was a very emotional story and it shows the relationship between a mother and her daughter and between the family.
