The comedy starts with showing a movie which is about future. Students are watching that movie. It is time to hand in homeworks but Bart didn't do his homework. So his teacher decides to send a letter to Bart's parent. Bart wants to intercept the letter but Simpson sees it. Simpson and Marge make Bart do his homework but in different way. Marge wants Bart study with enjoying his childhood. However Simpson wants Bart do his homework strictly. Bart abuse his parents behavior and alienate each other. Suddenly parents fight but end soon. After they make up they start to don't care about Bart. Bart finds old subway which wasn't used for several years. He finds out it can harm the city and even his school. He try to destroy his school but Simpson stops him with Bart's note. In Bart mind he didn't want to harm the city and his school.
