1 SUMMARY A keynote of Julian Bond reveals tension in the narration of the struggle for social justice and equal rights in America. They are two approches that are not mutually exclusive but distinctive. It's important to build a broad based coalition rather than focused on group identity and single group interests. Strategies that lead to success of modern movement for civil rights were litigation organisation mobilization and coalition.
