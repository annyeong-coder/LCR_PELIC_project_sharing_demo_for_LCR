C. What are some of the benefits of music Music has several benefits in life. First music can release our stress. Unfortunately modern people have a lot of stress. Besides some people have emotional disturbance and depression due to the stress. These days music is used to treat not only emotional disturbance and depression but also another mental illness. Second people can improve their concentration and convenience by music. For example when I work out I listen to music to overcome difficulty and boredom. Third people can decrease fear and worry. When dentists do dental treatment they use music for a patient to reduce their fear. Like this music gives many benefits to our life.
