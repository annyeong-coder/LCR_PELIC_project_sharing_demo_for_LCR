A prospector and his two dogs search for gold they get lost. They invaded sacred range by accident. They find a firm spot and fall into an uneasy sleep. The prospector dreams about fierce warrior and the warrior gives a hint to escape here. The prospector wakes up and finds glowing woman. After she transforms into a beautiful snow white rabbit leads him to solid ground and the rabbit became once more the beautiful feature. Then she vanishes as the first rays of the sun pierces horizon.
