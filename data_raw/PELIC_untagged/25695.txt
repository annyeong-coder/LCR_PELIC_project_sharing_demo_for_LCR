If you want to be a nurse specialist in Korea you must prepare many things. First of all graduate degree is mandatory because when you take a test for certification it is a major requirement for applicants. Graduate school is the only place which a nurse can learn and practice about your expertise knowledge such as infection control or emergency care. Next you have to have enough experience in your specialty. You can not be a nurse specialist just with its knowledge. Abundant medical experience is helpful not only to learn its knowledge well but also to apply it to patients accurately. Lastly English is also needed for nurse specialist because nowadays knowledge changes very quickly and most of newly published medical books or journals are written in English. If you want to be an excellent nurse specialist you have to have the capability which you can learn new knowledge through international journals or research. To be a nurse specialist is not easy but if you prepare those things above well you can make your dream come true.
