Have you ever eaten kimchi If your answer is yes you may be interested in Korean food. Kimchi is one of the best well known foods of Korea. Koreans eat kimchi almost every meal. Nowadays many people around the world know about kimchi and love kimchi. Here is the way to make kimchi. If you follow the next several steps it is not so difficult to make kimchi. The first and most important step in making kimchi is preparing many ingredients. There are many kind of kimchi but most of them have the common ingredients. These are the ingredients ok kimchi cabbages radishes garlic thick salts green onions dropwort ginger roots fish paste red chili pepper sticky rice paste surer oysters. The second step is making the filling which decides the taste of kimchi. Hence this step is very important. First cut the other vegetables such as radishes green onions and dropwort and chop the garlic and ginger roots. Second mix the picked fish paste red chili peppers sticky rice paste and salted oysters. Third Mix all those things. Then you can see the completed fillings. The last step is making and storing kimchi. First clean the cabbages and cut them into 2 4 pieces. Second soak them into salted water for 6 to 8 hours. In this step you need the thick salts. People often prepare the filling during this time gap. Third rinse them 3 times in running water and dry a bit. And then put the mixture the filling inside of the cabbages leaves scrupulously. Finally store the completed kimchi into a cool place and wait for a few days to make kimchi ripe. Making kimchi needs 2 or 3 days and requires many ingredients. It makes the process of making kimchi seem to be somewhat difficult. However as you see above once you prepare the ingredients and follow the steps it is easy to make kimchi. If you want more healthy and delicious food try to make kimchi. Maybe you will become to fall in love with kimchi.
