MY WEEKEND Last week I have a good weekend with my son. On sunday after breakfast we went to a play ground with my son. First we prepared some food and enough water. We left when we have prepared these. We were very glade in the sunlight. When we arrived the play ground there were many children. Oh my son saw tow children that were his friends.
