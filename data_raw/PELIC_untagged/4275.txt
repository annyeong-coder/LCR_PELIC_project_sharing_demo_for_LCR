Writhing5T 5 20 07 Topic What is a good paragraph A good paragraph has to have a topic sentence supporting sentence and concluding sentence. First the topic sentence is usually the first or the second sentence of a paragraph. It contains the subject of the paragraph and the controlling idea to show what is the main idea. Second the supporting sentence gives some evidence and examples to support the main idea. Third the concluding sentence is restating the main idea. A good paragraph can't without topic sentence supporting sentence and concluding sentence these three kinds of sentences.
