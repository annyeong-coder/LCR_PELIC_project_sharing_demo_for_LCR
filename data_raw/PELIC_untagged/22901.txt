The writers are talking about that how the children's creativity is limited and hindered. As a good example they showed the story of little Teresa Amabile who was blocked her creativity in the field of art by evaluation pressure one of the creativity killers which are surveillance rewards competition and so on. The magnificent thing to develop child's creativity is the time. However even though children have a good chance to evolve their creativity in proper time the rhythms of adults let their children quit the chance to expand their creativity in the middle of process. It is so natural that children begin to develop and ingest their creativity. Therefore adults should help them not to get lost what they really want to do and help get ride on the flow which is ultimate state of creativity with their rhythm instead of adults' rhythm.
