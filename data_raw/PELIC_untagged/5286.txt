North Korea's National Security Strategy In the article Dr. John Merrill who is a chief of the Northeast Asia Division in the U. S. Department explains North Korea's National Security Strategy. According to him there are four strategies economic revival national Security strategy popular support for the regime and strong military. First economic revival is important because North Korea's economic has shrunk dramatically. Next since North Korea is located between strong countries their foreign policy is important. Also they should be legitimate with South Korea. In addition popular support for regime is considered very essential element among North Korea elites. That is because they used nationalism to unite North Koreans but today performance is more important than nationalism. Finally strong military is important but it does not exist alone.
