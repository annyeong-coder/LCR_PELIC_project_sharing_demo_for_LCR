Instead of the thought of the single intelligence in the past Dr. Howard Gardner has advocated a new theory called Multiple Intelligence MI in 1983. In MI theory Gardner defines intelligence is a group of abilities which includes verbal linguistic logical mathematical visual spatial bodily kinesthetic musical rhythmic interpersonal and intrapersonal. Gardner asserted that generally these seven abilities are not exhaustive years latter he added the eighth ability naturalistic intelligence. He also claimed that every person has these 8 abilities however the proportion of these intelligences to an individual is different.
