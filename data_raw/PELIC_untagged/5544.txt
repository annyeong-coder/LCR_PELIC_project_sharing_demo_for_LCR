Then glue one molding strip to each side piece 14 down from top. The next step is to nail side pieces to bottom. And then nail the top in place. Next nail the back piece in place. The eighth step is to paint the bookcase and shelf. Before last step let dry 2 hours. Finally slide the shelf into place. If you work like these you could make easily a bookcase.
