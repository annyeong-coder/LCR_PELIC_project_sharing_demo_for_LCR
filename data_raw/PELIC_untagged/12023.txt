At the end of the 19th century there was one lady who was trying to tell her story. This story happened when the ice storm which was extremely strong hit the ship and broke it down into small pieces. A man met a lady and they started a relationship. They started a big story which was really wonderful. He was poor and she was rich which made them an odd couple. They were trying to hide this relationship from the people. At the same time the storm that damaged the ship was really harsh. Both of the characters try to survive. The result was awful because the ship crashed in the sea. Hundreds of people died and the ship sank which was a really bad ending for this story. The characters both of whom end up in the water try to wait for help. The male character died while his girlfriend survived.
