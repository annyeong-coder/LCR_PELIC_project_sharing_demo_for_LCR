Cohn has researched Koko language for 13 years. He purposed Koko learn language suing sigh language. Koko learned to use sigh language expressing drink and eat then more and more. Since 1976 Penny and Koko have lived in the wild. Koko learned how to use sigh language to express the complex emotion like respect trust and love. Koko liked to read books and look pictures from the books. She also liked to hear stories that penny read to her. She's beloved kid was a cat.
