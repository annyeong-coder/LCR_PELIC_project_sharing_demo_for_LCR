This text tells us how Annette Martin uses her abilities to help find missing people. She can see what happens and successfully help treat the cases that the police failed to treat in traditional methods. Rich Keaton who was a detective to cooperate with Martin has strongly believed in her and depended on her in many cases since he met her when she used her vision of murder to help them arrest the suspect. However a Skeptic Joe Nickell don't believe in her and explain that she is just a highly imaginative and emotional person according to real scientific studies. Lastly psychic' power will go on incurring different opinions.
