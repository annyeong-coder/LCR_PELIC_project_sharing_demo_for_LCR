Setting the right goals an ELI student might have her own goals for studying English. For instance if she wants to take a Tofel test she should realize the content of the test and be honest to her weakness then work out. Creating mind movies an ELI stdent could use this advice on her speaking class. For example when she prepares a speech which will be given in the class she can conjure up the situation such as time control eye connect limbs movement and the questions which her audiences might ask. It will be helpful for presenting the real speeches.
