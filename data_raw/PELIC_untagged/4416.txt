Jackie Oweis Sawiris is an Arab American director who produced the movie Falls the Shadow . She strongly believes that Arab Americans have a responsibility to change the images of Arab in US. Her intent to make this film is to brake stereotype to help Jordanian film industry become one of the international film community and consequently to change the images of Arabs by using showing To achieve this great purpose she got over various travails. She shot her film at all over country. She experienced working with Stanley Kubrick and held a bachelor's degree in journalism. She gathered professional team and characters from both Middle East and the West. Finally the author says that heart of the movie is Jordan itself for Swairis reflected her wards The movie is entirely about the country I felt in love with.
