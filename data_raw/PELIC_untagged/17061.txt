Being a student has a marked pressure on daily life. However the student may encounter some difficulties and problems. First of all selecting the appropriate major is the relevant concern for all students. If the course is hard he may have many classes and too much homework. At the same time he should manage his time to do his duties and study hard to pass the exams. Secondly financial problems have a clear impact on studying way. For example paying the tuition will be very hard to the student in case of not having enough money. As a result of this situation he may need to look for a job to study and work in the same time. Finally he might have homesickness if he is away from his city and this will add more stress on his studying. In conclusion being a student is a temporary hard situation. On the other hand he should realize his duties to overcome them.
