Black color is very dangerous color to wear in summer. We have to see that all our work has done before the sun stars showing its effect. And also we have to take as much as liquids rather than solid food. And the most important thing is that if we have to go out in the heat we have to take care of our skin by applying some lotions and all precautions for our eyes also has to be taken.
