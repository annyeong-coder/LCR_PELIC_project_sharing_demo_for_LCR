Why Play Is Vital Play is important in everyone's life humans as well as animals. However in our present time adults rarely get involved in play. In the 15th century by comparison adults were involved in all kinds of play. The act of play can change a hostile attitude to a joyful friendly behavior. Following back some criminals' lives it has been discovered that individuals develop hostile and criminal nature because of sever play deprivation in their lives. Play can help lift mood and remove depression. The types of play are body play object play social play rough and tumble play spectator play and imaginative play. It has been found that animals and rats do not have fully grown brains if they were deprived of play. Play is similarly important in our lives. Studies have shown that while engaged in play people do not usually have strokes heart attacks or similar ailments. To live and feel better we should fill our lives with all kinds of play.
