This text talks about the importance of chili peppers all over the world and its role in cuisine and health. According to the text chili peppers have an important contribution in the medical field. Some studies have shown that capsaicin which is the active substance of chili peppers not only act as a vitamin complement but also as a pain controller. There exist other medical benefits that we can get from chili peppers such as nasal decongestant in common cold and the prevention of blood clots which are the principal cause of heart attack. Many countries are using chili peppers for cooking as well as health which are making them a worldwide phenomenon.
