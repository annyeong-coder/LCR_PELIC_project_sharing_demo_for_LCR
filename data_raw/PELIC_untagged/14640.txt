To get a driver's license in my country it should match with the following criteria. First you must be at least 18 years old. Drivers under the age of 18 can't apply for a driving test. Second you have to pass physical check up before a driving test. Then you have to pass knowledge test. Also you must pass one test is on site driving test at a official test center and the other test is driving test in a actual roads such as driving around a town. You must not fail any one of tests in order to have a driving license.
