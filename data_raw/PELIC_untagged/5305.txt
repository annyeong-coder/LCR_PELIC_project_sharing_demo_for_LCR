The advantage of the ELI at the University of Pittsburgh is facilities. For example it has an excellent computer lab. It has many windows and Mac computers and you can use these computers as if you were university student. When you would like to print out documents you can print for free at Hillman Library. In addition if you bring a laptop computer to campus you can connect wireless network of University of Pittsburgh. You can enjoy high speed internet surfing at campus and you can do online homework even though you do not have internet access at your home. Therefore facilities at the University of Pittsburgh lead you to profit to study in the ELI.
