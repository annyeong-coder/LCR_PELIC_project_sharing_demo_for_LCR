In the twenties alcohol was officially forbidden by the law but the law was not followed and everyone could find liquor from a local bar. In those years the cities were dominated by systematized crime and the police failed to prevent them. The Jazz became very popular all over the country and the classical music was ignored. The young recognized the men like Bix Biederbecke Louis Armstrong and Count Basie as heros. A best representation of the America's break with the past might be the flapper with bobbed hair and short skirts who was born in the twenties
