A Troubling Trend in Diabetes In this article the author emphasizing on a different type of diabetes which is not well known and implies that despite of the increasing number of kids diagnosed with type 2 diabetes we shouldn't forget type 1 diabetes since there are many evidences support this claim. Dr. Ranit mentioned a recent studies which were handled in Europe and supporting this fear. In addition to the previous studies a Similar study in the US shows that type 1 diabetes may increase faster than before due to several reasons. Then the author discusses the causes by pointing out what the researchers came to by looking for the causes of this trend in diabetes among children. In the last two paragraphs Dr. Ranit talked about the symptoms and how to prevent our children from type 1 diabetes.
