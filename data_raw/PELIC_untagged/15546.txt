In Thailand we have a lot of famous foods in our country. Such as Tom Yam Tom Kah Kiew Wan and Pad Thai. Pad Thai is national dish of Thailand. The ingredient of Pad Thai is Thai rice noodles shrimps tofu egg fish sauce lime garlic sugar chili pepper vegetable oil The way to make Pad Thai First soak the dry noodles in lukewarm water for 5 10 minutes. Then put oil in wok and use high heat. Add garlic and tofu and stir them until they start to brown. Drain the noodles and add I to the wok. Stir quickly and add sugar fish sauce lime chili pepper. After that put the egg in the wok and fold the egg into the noodles. Finally put shrimps and stir. Stir a few more times and finish the dish.
