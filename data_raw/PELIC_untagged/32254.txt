Normally it is a long and strict educational process of becoming a doctor. The education includes four years of Medical school and from three to eight years of residency training in speciality. Then a good doctor is really devoted to the job. For example it takes a lot of responsibility and they usually work overtime. Their years of experience working as a doctor are also a significant factor. Finally communication plays a big role between doctor and patient and doctors should have sense of human psychology. Certainly being polite kind and empathic is necessary. However it is not complicated to conclude that being a good doctor involves a huge spectrum of qualities.
