Title How to make the book shelf Generally speaking it is difficult to make book shelf by oneself. But making bookshelf is easy when you follow these steps. We need these assembles as followed. Wood nails glue a hammer a saw sandpaper and paint. First cut the wood into 2 side pieces 11 5 8 by 28 3 pieces for top bottom and shelf 11 5 8 by 13 1 2 1 back piece 15 by 28 two 10 molding strips and then sand each piece of wood. Second glue on molding strip to each side piece 14 down from top. Third nail side pieces to bottom the top and the back piece in place. After painting the bookcase and self we need to let it dry 2 hours. Finally slide the shelf into place. It is easy to make book shelf when you follow these steps. You don't need to spend your money for an expensive ready made bookshelf.
