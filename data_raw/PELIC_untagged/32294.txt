Religion perspectives in organ transplantation Most common question when a patient needs organ transplant does my religion and tradition accepts that. About thousand people die annually because they need organ transplants and the physician do not find donors. But nothing is worse than when a chance to donate an organ is missed because of the false belief that donation is prohibited by religion. With the revolution of medicine orang donation such as liver heart bone eyes lung and so on plays crucial roles in human live and survival. Although some religions and tradition oppose and against taking organ form those who have just died this belief should be abolished . Moreover there is some evidence that organ donation it is not considered to be violation of human.
