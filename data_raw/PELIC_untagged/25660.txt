Uganda my home country popularly known as the Pearl of Africa is located along the Nile Valley. Together with Kenya Tanzania Rwanda and Burundi we form the East African Community. The Equator an imaginary line dividing the earth into the northern and southern hemispheres passes through Uganda my country. It is one of the few East African countries with diverse cultures. For example it has about fifty cultural groups each with a distinct culture. But most of the Ugandan Cultural tribes marry more than one wife and pay bride price women are generally accepted a property to the husband they keep animals like goats sheep and cows belief in the existence of a supernatural being and speak many languages. Even though Ugandans got independence from Britain in 1962 even today we Ugandans are still struggling with the beginning stages of development.
