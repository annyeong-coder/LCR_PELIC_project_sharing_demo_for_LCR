In this article The creative spirit the author discuses about the creativity of children. He provides an example of creativity who is Teresa. He tells her story of creativity when she was a child especially in her first school and what happen when she transferred to new school. The author provides a lot of creativity obstacles in the articles such as surveillance evaluation and reward. He acknowledged that all of these obstacles deprive the children form creativity. He considers the time one of the serious creativity killers because it is subtle or intangible. He believes that children have more naturally than the adults. On the other hand they cannot attribute their creativity because the interruption of adults. For instance Children want the chance to follow their natural inclinations. In conclusion the author believes children can be creative without any obtrusion.
