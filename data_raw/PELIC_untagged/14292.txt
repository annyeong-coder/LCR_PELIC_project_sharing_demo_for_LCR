Nowadays there is new type of disease due to develop of technology. It is the video game addiction. This situation has affected especially for children and teenagers. The people addicted the video game tend to give up everything about their life instead of to concentrate the games. The their relationship in family and society have been broken because they do not care anymore since they are addicted. The reason why rate of this disease is becoming higher constantly is complex. Generally the someone might think his life was fail could be addicted more than others. For solving this problem there are several ways. First someone who tends to be merely inside the house should be bring out of the house. Introducing various sports to them might change their view to video game. Secondly there is way to take the patients in mental hospital. If the patient is addicted seriously experts about this types of patients must be known the way to cure for them.
