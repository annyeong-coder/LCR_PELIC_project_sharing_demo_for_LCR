Title A Cruel and Unusual Record This article is about the human rights in the U. S. Before and after September 11 2001 attacks. It stated how the principles of human right had changed. Then the writer talked deeply about rights of freedom justice and peace in the world. Next he talked about international human rights and how the U. S. Offensively deals with some countries. Finally from the writer perspective he thinks Americans should encourage their government to change the way they treat international problems in order to apply human rights in the world.
