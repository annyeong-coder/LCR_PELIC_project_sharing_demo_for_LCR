One of The Saudi proverb is Home is not where you born in but where you get paid from . Home is not necessarily refers to house which is an alternative meaning but it may refer to city or even job. In Saudi Arabia where I had been raised people are family oriented. So they prefer to work at the city where they live in. When someone receives a job offer from company in a different area he she usually hesitates about travelling to that area. Therefore surroundings especially parents sometimes insist on going to whom receive the job's offer to encourage him her to work there. Ultimately they said that famous proverb Home is not where you born in but where you get paid from .
