Juraj Janosik is considered one of the greatest hero in the history of Slovakia. He was a famous outlaw also described as the Slovak Robin Hood. It is known that he has been topic of many slovak and polish legends books and films. After battle of Trencin he created a forrest robber group and he was the leader. It is also believed that he robbed nobles and gave the loot to the poor. Most of their victims were rich merchants. According a legend he was caught on spilled peas. He was sentenced to death. Our national hero is regarded as the most brave and strong man who wanted to change bad land in his country.
