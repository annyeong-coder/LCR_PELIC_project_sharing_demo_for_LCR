Sports are rich and colorful and they have different types of styles. We can separate them as team sports and individual sports. Team sports need team members to cooperate in harmony. Just depending on one person can't win the game. Some team sports need huge fields such as baseball football and soccer so they need more team members. There are total 22 players in a football game. Well other team sports' field is smaller. They are basketball volleyball and ice hockey. The individual sports are different skill is important. There are two kinds. One is a two player game and another is a many player game. Tennis boxing ping pong and badminton belong to two player games. Many player games also have many styles. They are marathon horse racing gymnastics ice skating bowling and golf. Every sport is interesting and you will like them if you often watch the games. Choose one sport you like and enjoy it.
