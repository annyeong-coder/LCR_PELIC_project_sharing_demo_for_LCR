In this interview Charlie Rose hosts the German actor Christophe Waltz to talk about his new movie Inglorious Basterds. Waltz tells us how he got the role how he met Quentine the director and the writer of the movie. Besides Waltz talks about the script and the audition and how he utilized from his classes with Stella Adler in his training for the movie. Finally he tells us how he is grateful for this chance which is considered a transmit in his career as an actor and what this experience has added to him what he has learnt from working with Quentine .
