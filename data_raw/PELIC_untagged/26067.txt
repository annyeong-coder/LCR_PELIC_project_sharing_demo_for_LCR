The Good Boss There are three required advantages to be a good boss. First at all the good boss should has the experience. He ought to work at least three years in his major. Second the good boss must be able to control his employees. He is able to make the decision in difficult situations. Third the good boss has personal skills. For example he need sometimes to have sense of humor to make his employees more comfortable with their work. In conclusion the good boss has knowledge leadership and sense of smart personal touch on his work.
