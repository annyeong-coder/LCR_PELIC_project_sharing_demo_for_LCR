Prepare ingredients Meat Lamb Onion Garlic Salt Rice Water Prepare Purpose Pot Knife Spoon Cup Dish I will write the method of preparation Kabsa that enough for four people . First I cut one onion medium size three lobes of garlic and two tomatoes big size as well as cut the meat to medium pieces. Second I put a little oil in the pot then add onions and a little stir it for two minutes . Next add garlic tomatoes meat and stir it for three minutes. Then add five cups water to the meat and two teaspoon of salt and close the lid of the pot and let it on quiet fire for 20 minutes . After that talk of the lid and add two cups of rice and close the lid again for 10 minutes . Finally. Close the fire and it will be ready to eating .
