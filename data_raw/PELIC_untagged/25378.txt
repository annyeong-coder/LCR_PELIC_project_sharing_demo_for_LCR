Titanic is a romantic American film which was written by James Cameron. It stars Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet who play the main role in the movie. The movie released in December 1997 became very popular all over the world. This movie is about Kate and Jack two young people from different social classes who fall in love with each other. Jack who is a very poor artist wins a ticket to the trip in a game. Kate who is going to Philadelphia to marry her rich boyfriend feels helpless and tries to kill herself but Jack rescues her. They become friends after that. Rose invites Jack to have dinner with her and her friends most of whom don't respect Jack because he is from the third class.
