The Earth is waiting our response Recycling reducing and reusing are ways through which people can produce less wastes and can also be economically profitable. These ways are not a waste of time money and energy. Personally I have never recycled and to me the Earth's resources seemed endless. Goofy thinking isn't it Of course we will run out of natural resources eventually which is scary. So the question is how do we get use to recycling to make our valuable resources last longer The three most commonly proposed solutions are to educate people for recycling make it easier to recycle and create more reasonable compensation system or rules to encourage people to recycle. As I said I never recycle. I had less acquisition about recycling till I came to the States. Truthfully in Mongolia nobody talks and cares about recycling. Most of us never even heard about this issue. It is evident that people definitely need to learn the benefits of recycling and why we need to do it. People need to be aware of why it is crucial. Local governments should make it a community ordeal unify and indicate residents' perspectives through this issue. They should evaluate and encourage the public to recycle through television radio and newspapers. Good demonstration could modify the community's initial perspectives and could give them a chance to evaluate the crucial factors of limited natural resources or recycling. Recycling wastes does not need to be a complex thing to do. The majority of people are running busily for something. For this reason people usually do not want to spend more time categorizing wastes or finding the places where recycled wastes are collected. Some say that it is a waste of time and money. It is evident that we definitely need an easier method to recycle in efficient way. Increasing the number of places where recycled wastes are collected may help. Close to residential areas or garbage pickup service would be helpful. People would not have to worry about driving around looking for places to drop the recycled wastes. Maybe it could be a hidden way to encourage and motivate people to recycle. It is very obvious that in today's society almost no one works for free even if it is in their best interest. Creating a compensation system could be the method to achieve a good consequence. My neighbor Mr. Santos recycles his wastes. He usually mentions recycling to me but his implications did not affect my philosophy. Recycling and its benefits are not exciting to me. However if I heard that I would get paid to recycle maybe I would have done it a long time ago! Compensation could trigger people to recycle. Although it is complex people need to transform their mind and habit to recycling. To sustain the Earth's potential people should involve themselves in this issue. Today's global trend indicates that the availability of natural resources is getting less. Individuals local government and the community should involve themselves with this facing problem. They should work together to enhance the range of people who recycle. Otherwise we could face serious issues such as lack of consumption in the future.
