Sports are really important to us Since ancient times sports have closely related to our lives. Many basic sports like running swimming throwing and wrestling were inspired to make good soldier. But nowadays sports have many good aspects for our society. At first sports make our body healthy. Unlike old days people don't need to move their body in order to get meals which make people weaker. If they play sports regularly they can be stronger than who don't play sports. Secondly through sports game they can increase challenging power. While they challenge for win in the games over and over they become not give up any difficulties. Thirdly they can participate and feel pleasure and sorrow even if they just watch the games. The feeling of pleasure and sorrow also make people active.
