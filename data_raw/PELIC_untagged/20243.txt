Family Vacation When I decide to have a family vacation I should consider many factors when planning the vacation. First I consider the interest of everyone. For Example I try to have the place that everyone likes such as the Beach good restaurants or fun activities. Second I consider the duration of the vacation. For example We should plan enough time to do all things that we like to do. Third I consider the contributions that we need. For example we may need some water food gas or a tent. Indeed to have a good family vacation you should consider the important factors to be successful on your vacation.
