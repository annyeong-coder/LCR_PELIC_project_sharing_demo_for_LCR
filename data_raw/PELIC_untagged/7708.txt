People in my community live as tribes. They are located southern of the Kingdome. Each tribe has a leader. The leader is related to his tribe I mean he has to be one of the tribe. He has to have some adjectives. First he has to be educated. Second he must be known about his honesty fairness and judgment. He is chosen by his tribe. He is regarded as an important person. They believe that his knowledge and wisdom could lead to live in a peace and quietude. Moreover what he says is considered as valuable words by his tribe and other tribes. Therefore he is composed his speeches carefully. Each leader is connected to other by exchange gifts. It is thought by some clans that this habit has benefits for their people. The sheik is respected by his tribe and other sheiks. Finally he is surrounded by his sons in every event.
