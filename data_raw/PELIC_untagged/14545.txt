There are 2 type of oils oils solid fats. Oils are fats that are liquid at room temperature like canola oil corn oil cottonseed oil olive oil safflower oil soybean oil and sunflower oil. Solid fats are fats that are solid at room temperature like butter beef fat chicken fat pork fat stick margarine and shortening. All oils and fat contain fatty acids. Solid fats have more saturated fats trans fats which are not good for health than oil. Oils also have essential fatty acids that are necessary for health. However we need to calculation about consumption because oils are still have 120 calories per tablespoon. A woman needs 5 or 6 tablespoon of oils per day a man needs 6 or 7 tablespoon of oils per day.
