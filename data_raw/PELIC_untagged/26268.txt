In this talk Cameron Herold told us about how to raise kids to be entrepreneurs . Being entrepreneur is same as being doctor or lawyer. They all have a talent which need to care. He grew up in the entrepreneurial family. So he was taught to have his own business. He started his business at the age of 7 and learn skills to be good entrepreneur. He gave some pieces of advises such as teach not to waste money stand up in the front of a group and talk. In addition there are some entrepreneur traits need to nurture in kids like attainment sales and tenacity. Anyone can be entrepreneur.
