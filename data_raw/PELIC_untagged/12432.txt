Causes of childhood obesity The subject of child and adolescent obesity become more popular in the United States because of the radical increase of the disease. Nowadays 25 of the children and adolescent of the country are obese. There are many factors that contribute to obesity among children bad eating habits and sedentary lifestyle are the two main reasons. Obesity occurs when a person eat more calories than the body can burn it. Basically when comparing the nutritional factor with the calories of the food we will notice that certain types of food have more calories and fewer nutrients than others. This group of food is more common among the children's taste. Some examples are fast food soft drinks candies and more.
