How to Make Ice Cream im a Bag Surprise your friends on your next camping trip by making ice cream in a plastic bag. First put 1 2 cup of heavy cream 1 2 cup of milk 1 4 cup of sugar and 1 4 teaspoon of vanilla into a quart size sealable plastic bag. Then close the quart size bag and seal it tightly. The next step is to put 2 cups of ice and 1 2 to 3 4 cup of salt into a gallon size sealable plastic bag and to place the sealed quart bag inside the gallon bag of ice and salt. After that seal the gallon bag securely. For ten to fifteen minutes or until the contents of quart bag have turned into ice cream gently rock the gallon bag from side to side. Finally open the bags grap a spoon and enjoy your ice cream!
