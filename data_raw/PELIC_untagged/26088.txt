This episode is showing the first week in HGTV design star. The completion takes place in New York city. There is 12 contenders compete to have final prize which is design show. There are two criteria which must be in consideration of each contender. First design ability which shows ability to design bed room for partner depend on their conditions and what he she likes or dislike. In this task the contender will have 500 and he or she will go shopping in Asian shop in one hour in order to design the white box as bed room. The judgment will be based on the inspiration and evaluation from partner as well as judgments. Eventually they will be top ten and bottom ten and from the bottom ten they will be bottom two. The bottom two must show presentation about their work. By the end of this presentation the judgment will reveal to eliminate one designer.
