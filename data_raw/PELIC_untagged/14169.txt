Cooking a dish Aseda Ingredients of Aseda I. Cup of flour. II. Cup and half water. III. Salt as you like. IV. Butter or olive oil. V. Honey or liquid of dates. The recipe There are several steps to prepare Aseda. First put the water in a pot. Second put the pot on the fire and put the salt in a pot. Then put the flour gradually in the water during boil. Next stir the amount for half an hour until fully mature. After a maturity design it like a circle and place it in the bowl. Finally pour some honey or a liquid of dates. Focus the steps in order to help you how to prepare.
