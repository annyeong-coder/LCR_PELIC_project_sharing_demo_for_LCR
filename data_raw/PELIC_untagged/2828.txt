Attention! A jewelry store in Downtown Pittsburgh was robbed this morning. The two thieves were arrested while they were driving to Oakland. They intended to escape but the police was alerted and after two hours of persecution the thieves were caught and arrested. After that the thieves were conduced to the Police Station and there they were interrogated and interviewed by the authorities. Finally the thieves confessed that they had taken and stolen five diamond rings and they would sell them in other country. Moreover it is assumed that one of the salesmen could have been involved in the robbery. The police investigated the security of the jewelry store and made some questions to the owner who accused a salesman. The new salesman could have watched the entrance of the thieves and permitted the robbery said the owner. After all the owner received the rings again and he promised to guard them better.
