When Yolanda was about 3 year old she can draw a crowd by reciting poem on the bus and she didn't even noticed that her parents were not on the bus. From this situation her mother saw that it's an innate gift that she has. Since her mother went to all of Yolanda's poetry reading she's certain that she'll be a famous poet. Many years after her graduate school Yolanda didn't write much poem like she used to instead she turned to become a schoolteacher and told her family that she was not a poet anymore. However she still have chance to recite poetry and her mother never miss any of her performance. Her mother feels very proud of her and know that she can never resist what she really is.
