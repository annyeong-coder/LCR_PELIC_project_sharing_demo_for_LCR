The banality of evil is a phrase that summarize the result of The Milgram Experiment . In fact banality of evil denote how commonplace the wrong doing is. In other words according to that experiment most people are willing to torture other people depending on the circumstances. This phrase is strange and unusual because people believe that most people who are assumed to be good are not able to behave extremely wrong. The Milgram Experiment shows that ordinary people can conscientiously participate in a terrible destructive process in order to obey authority.
