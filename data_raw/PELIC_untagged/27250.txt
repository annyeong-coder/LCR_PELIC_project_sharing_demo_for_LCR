Used tires have many usages one of which is to use them to control and prevent soil erosion. In the western part of the country rainfalls are short but very strong and heavy which result in soil erosion. To prevent this rocks stones and concrete can be used. However they are expensive difficult to haul and inconvenient. In contrast used tires are generally free and so much easier to carry. So they could be placed under the ground in a certain way that could retain the soil and prevent it from eroding. Concrete n a substance used for building that is made by mixing sand water small stones and CEMENT. Haul v to carry or pull sth heavy retain v to keep sth or to continue to have sth.
