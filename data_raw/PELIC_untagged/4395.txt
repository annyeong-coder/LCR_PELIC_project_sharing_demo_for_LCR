Title Nuclear Tremors in Asia Summary North Korea successfully tested a nuclear bomb on October 9 2006 despite concerned neighbors in Asia as well as the ongoing concern of the USA. As a result the UN Security Council passed tough sanctions such as an export embargo and severely limited the flow of luxury goods into the nation. To get a pledge to refrain from further nuclear tests the USA wants to meet a representative at the six party meetings but this appears to be very difficult. Other neighboring countries are anxious for talks regarding nuclear programs to resume but Japan and the USA are taking a hard line. Japan is even making noises about the possibility of starting its own nuclear program. Two words Sanction n Official order that limits trade contact etc. With a particular country in order to make it do something such as obeying international law Embargo n An official order that forbids trade with another country
