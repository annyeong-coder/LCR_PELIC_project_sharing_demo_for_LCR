The Statue of Librty The Statue of Librty was dedicated of October 28 1886. It was given to the United State by the people of France to represent the friendship between the two countries. The Statue of Librty is standing on Librty Island in New York Harbor it welcome visitors traveling by ship. The statue is 151 ft tall but with the pedestal and foundation it is 305 ft tall. The Statue of Librty is one of the most recognizable symbols on the world.
