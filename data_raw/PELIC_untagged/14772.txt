There are a lot of interesting things to see and do in New York City. It is home to over 150 world class museums. There are art museums science museums photography museums natural history museums and even a museum of seaport history. New York is known for their rich variety of theater music and dance. From the bright lights of Broadway and the respected stages at Lincoln Center and Carnegie Hall to the high kicks of the Rocketed at Radio City Music Hall and incredible jazz at intimate clubs there is something for everyone. Many people go to New York because the wonderful restaurants. There are thousands of restaurants to please every palate and wallet if you are looking for a place to shop. You will find everything you can imagine. With more than 10 000 shops filled with brand names and bargains from around the world NYC is a shopper's paradise. As for me people watching are my favorite New York pastime.
