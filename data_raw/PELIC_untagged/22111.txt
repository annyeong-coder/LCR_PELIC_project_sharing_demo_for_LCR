Saudi Arabia is located in the Middle East. It is boarded by Iraq and Syria from the north Yemen from the south the Red sea from the west and Kuwait Qatar and Oman from the east. The capital city of Saudi Arabia is Riyadh . Saudi Arabia is the largest exporter of petroleum in the world. Saudi Arabia is mostly made up of deserts. As a result the weather in Saudi Arabia is hot in the summer and cold in the winter. Finally Saudi Arabia is considered to be a conservative country. People over there respect their customs and traditions. At the same time they are open to the world.
