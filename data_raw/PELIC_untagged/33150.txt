Every year there are more than 1000 deaths due to bicycling. 75 Of deaths had head injuries which consider to be the major cause of death. Fifty percent of those victims are School age children. There is a Study shows that the incidence of Head injury can decrease by eighty five percent by a bike helmet. Bike helmet works as shock absorber and protects the head when an accident happens.
