Both texts The Biggest Trees on Earth and Up a Tree are similar in their topic yet different in the content. The first text contains information and records from scientists about the Biggest and oldest trees that have been discovered. It also focuses on the look shape and measured sizes of the trees. The text style looks like an article or a news writing. On the other hand the second text contains a first hand experience of a girl with big trees. The focus is on the girls view of the trees shape and size. This text is written in a style of a story or a personal experience.
