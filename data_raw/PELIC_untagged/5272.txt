In my opinion I think the ELI at the University of Pittsburgh is a good choice for students who are planning to study English in the U. S. For example there is a Language Media Center in the ELI. The Language Media Center offer many services and facilities for studying English. You can use audio and video cassettes for training your listening. You can use videotape for conversion. Also there are software for computer assisted and web enhanced language Learning. In the Language Media Center there is a special systems in the computer for training in Speaking and Grammar class. You can use computer do Recorded Speaking Activity and Grammar Recorded Speech activities. These equipments are very useful and helpful for studying English. The Language Media Center of the ELI offer a environment for improve your studying English quickly.
