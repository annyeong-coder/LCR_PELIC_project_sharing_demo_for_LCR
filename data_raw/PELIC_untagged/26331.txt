Eid Al fitr is an Islamic holiday. Muslims cerebrate this day after fasting during the holy month of Ramadan. Muslim are not only cerebrating the end of fasting but thanking Allah for the help and strength that he gave them throughout the previous month to help them practice self control. Eid Al fitr begins in first Shawwal every year which is month following Ramadan. Shawwal is the tenth month of islamic calender. There are some preparing before and during this day.
