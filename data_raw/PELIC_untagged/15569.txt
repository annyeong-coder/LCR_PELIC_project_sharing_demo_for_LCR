People are suffering from insomnia have trouble time falling asleep or staying asleep for an adequate amount of time. One great solution for this problem is to exercise regularly. Research has shown that regular exercise is an effective solution. People experiencing insomnia should exercise regularly to help them falling asleep. Also they should exercise in the afternoon or early evening to get the most benefit out of it. Moreover they should not exercise before going to bed. This is one of the best solution to insomnia.
