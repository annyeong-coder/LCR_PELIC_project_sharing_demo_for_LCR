This article is written by GRETCHEN REYNOLDS in May 23 2012 talks about marathon runners researches to find true results about how much marathon effect on runners and sometimes causes death to them. In general research prove that the older runners more tend to heart attack after 24 hours from marathon. On the other hand some researches prove that unhealthy lifestyle also consider as a main factor for these heart attack. Also a lot of runners says that they gain benefits from this activity that is prevent them from heart attack. The most important findings from all these researches prove that the percentage of getting heart attack when you are sitting or walking and relaxing is more than risk in marathon.
