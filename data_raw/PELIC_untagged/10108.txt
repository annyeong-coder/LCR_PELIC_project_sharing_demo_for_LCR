They conducted three surveys in about 50 countries using questions about 30 characteristics that fall under the five larger categories neuroticism extraversion openness agreeableness and conscientiousness. They asked the volunteers to rate their own personalities the first survey rate the personalities of other individuals they knew the second survey and describe a typical person from their culture the third survey . They then compared the three profiles that they got from the volunteers to confirm if they are related each other or not.
