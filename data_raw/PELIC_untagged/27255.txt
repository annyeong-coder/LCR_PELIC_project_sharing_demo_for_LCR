There are lots of ways that we can recycle old socks. Before using old socks be sure to clean them thoroughly especially when you use them as dog pull toy and dish washing glove. Besides old socks can be also used as CD mailers which prevent CD from getting scratched. They can be used as car washing glove car wax buffer and dusting glove too. Recycle your old socks and you won't feel bad about throwing away old socks anymore!
