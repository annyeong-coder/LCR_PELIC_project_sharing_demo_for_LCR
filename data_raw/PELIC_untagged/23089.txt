A walk to remember is adaptation from bestseller book by Nicholas Sparks. Landon he is popular in his high school opposite to Jamies she is very quite and always wears the same sweater all the time. Their story start when there is one guy wanna to join Landon's group. He forced that guy to jump into a lake. Unfortunately that guy got hurt and Landon was the only one who got caught. So Landon has to join janitorial staff after school by tutoring on Saturday and take part in drama club which are the same activities as Jamies. Landon plays Tommy a club owner and Jamies plays Alicia a singer in his club. During practicing a play they're falling in love.
