Coversation with Marie Antoinette The Reporter asked Marie how she became the Queen of France and Marie responded that when she was fourteen her mother told her that she had go to France to live in the French court. She did not know that she had to marry Louis XIV. The Reporter asked Marie how she felt about it then and she answered that she loved Paris. She said that it was so liveled there and beautiful than Germany. The Reporter told Marie that the poor were complaining that they had no food to eat and so she asked her what she had to say about that. Marie said that let them eat cake. The Reporter asked Marie is she thought they would react well to that statement and she pointed out that she didn't care what they thought. She add that she was the queen.
