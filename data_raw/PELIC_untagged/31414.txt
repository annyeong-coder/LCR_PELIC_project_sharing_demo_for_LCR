First beat the eggs with a few grinding of sea salt a few more of pepper add a tablespoon of white wine and a handful of Parmigiono cheese Next twirl frying pan to coat it with a few drops of oil and let on quiet warm After that drop in blossoms whole flatten them a bit so they stay put and leave them for minute or so while tear a few basil leaves and give the eggs another stroke or two. Then throw a few fennel seeds to scent the oil and beginning to take color. Next wait time to increase the heat and add the eggs batter. After that run the little cake under a hot grill to form a gold skin on top Finally slide it onto a plate and sprinkling torn basil on top.
