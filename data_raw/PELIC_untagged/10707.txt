Kicking Butt and smoking in Public live and let live are alike in the aspect of unusual view of smoking both of them point out the problem of anti smokers and non smokers they neglect the smoker's right. They are different in the following aspects in the article of Kicking Butt the author cites some research report and authority such as FDA bill emphasizes that the goal anti smoking should be save lives not to extinguish tobacco or make companies lose and utilizes tobacco through reengineering such as developing vaccine while in the article of smoking in Public live and let live the author mainly illustrates his own idea through comparing smokers and non smokers tries to let readers confound smoking with other things.
