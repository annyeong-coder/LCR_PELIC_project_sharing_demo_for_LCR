It is time to prepare for London Olympics games 2012. The torch is around the corner to be carried in the torch relay which will start on Friday until the game start on July 23. The torch is made of aluminum alloy skin. It is perforated by 8 000 circles representing the 8 000 torchbearers who will carry the flame. The color of the torch is gold. It will be traveled through 1 019 cities towns and villages and visit landmarks. It is come from Greece where hosted the first Olympics games.
