An area of woods was burned and two polices found a dead body. Then they called NCIS. NCIS collected evidences and analyzed them. After that they found the poison track in the body and knew that someone adapted the jet pack which was belong to the pilot that dead body. Therefore NCIS interrogated the jet pack company's owners Mr. And Mrs. Tillman. They found that pilot Sayers was hired by Tillman to test jet packs but Sayers stole their invention. Sayers also asked his partner to help him producing copy one because of his engineering background. However his partner murdered him for stealing the jet pack and then used controller to make Sayers and his jet pack crashed in the woods after he died. Finally NCIS arrested the murderer.
