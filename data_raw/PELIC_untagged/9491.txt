Giuseppe Garibaldi is considered an italian hero. He is known as the maker of the Italy's Unity. It is reported that on may the 6th 1860 Garibaldi sailed from Quarto a village located in the north of Italy to Marsiglia a Sicily port. He was accompained by 1089 man who are belived to be voluntaries. From then they have been colled mille that means one thousand . This expedition is said to be decisive for the Unity of Italy. During the battles against the Bourbonist it is reported that Garibaldi had a leg injured but he never gave up. He has been always supported by the local population and he could finally free the south of Italy from the Borbounist so that in 1861 the United Italy could have her firts constitution.
