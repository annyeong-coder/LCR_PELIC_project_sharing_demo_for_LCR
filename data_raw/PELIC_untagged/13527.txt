Ms. Mason gave us tips about how to eliminate awkwardness during a conversation. The best way to generate a good conversation is to ask wh questions such as What when where what if etc. Another useful phrase to carry on a conversation is tell me more . Besides wh questions it's also good to ask people about their hobby where they live what are their favorite sports etc. She also mentioned that it is crucial to listen when others are answering your questions. Never talk on top of others' speaking.
