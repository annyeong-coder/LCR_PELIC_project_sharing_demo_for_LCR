Surprise your friends on your next camping trip by making ice cream in a plastic bag. First put 1 2 cup of heavy cream 1 2 cup of milk 1 4 cup of sugar and 1 4 teaspoon of vanilla into a quart size sealable plastic bag. Then close the quart size bag and seal it tightly. Second place the sealed quart bag inside the gallon bag of ice and salt. Next put 2 cups of ice and 1 2 to 3 4 cup of salt into a gallon size sealable plastic bag. The third step is seal the gallon bag securely. Finally gently rock the gallon bag from side to side for ten to fifteen minutes or until the contents of the quart bag have turned into ice cream. To sum up open the bags grap a spoon and enjoy your ice cream!
