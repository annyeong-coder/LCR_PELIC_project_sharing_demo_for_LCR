Leonardo da Vinci is assumed to be one of the greatest genius of humanity. Although he is best known as a painter he is regarded as a sculptor an architect an engineer a mathematician an anatomist a musician and an inventor. It is considered to be the precursor of the geology. Lots of his discoveries in astronomy and botany are thought to be revolutionary. It is also assumed that he prefigured recent inventions like the parachute the bicycle the helicopter and the car.
