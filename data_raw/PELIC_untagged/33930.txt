A second benefit to studying English in the ELI is you can enjoy many activities in the city of Pittsburgh. Pittsburgh is a big city and it has many interesting things to do. It also has different activities in each season. For example in the summer you can enjoy water activities such as canoeing fishing and sailing. In the winter you can enjoy snow and also you can do many winter activities such as skiing tubing and ice skating. In addition Pittsburgh has sports and popular sports teams in each season. In the summer they have the Pirates team to play baseball. For fall they have the Steelers team to play football. In the winter they have the Penguins team to play ice hockey. If you don't like outdoors activities you can enjoy indoors activities. Pittsburgh has many interesting museums including the Carnegie Museum of Natural History Carnegie Museum of Art and Carnegie Science Center.
