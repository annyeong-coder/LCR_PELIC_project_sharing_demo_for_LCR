There are some advices for a new ELI student to succeed in learning English. First of all you should prepare yourself next day's lessons and review today's lessons. In addition you never fail to do homework. Next you should ask a teacher imediately when you have a question. Then you make a good friend in a class to exchange your ideas or decide something. Also it is important to have many chances to talk with English speakers as much as possible. Last of all while living in the U. S. A. It is better for you to talk in English with your family too.
