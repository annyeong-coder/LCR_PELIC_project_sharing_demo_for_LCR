You can raise your English skills by watching television. When you go back to your home you can not use English if you do not have American roommates or foreign friends. Thus watching television can help you. First of all it will improve your listening because you can hear various pronunciations and accents from different channels or programs such as news movies and cartoons. Moreover you can learn about conversation which use in real life. Finally if you open subtitle you can practice your reading and may find new vocabulary which you do not know before. It is your opportunity to learn English by yourself at home.
