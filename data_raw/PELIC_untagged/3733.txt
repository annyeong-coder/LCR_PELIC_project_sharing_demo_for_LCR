Veganism The diet consists on getting the protein from fruits beans grains and vegetables and staying completely away from meat fish poultry eggs and dairy products. Although an ethical issue about protecting the animal rights and the environment is the main cause for people adhering to this diet many health benefits have been discovered in the recent years. Veganism an extension of the vegetarian diet is considered an integral component of a cruelty free lifestyle Vegan Action . Nowadays many studies support that the consumption of animal fat and proteins are the main cause for many lethal diseases such as cancer diabetes heart attacks obesity and some others. Therefore some people choose to follow this diet for health reasons. It seems almost unexplainable that all the fat and proteins found in animal products can be also found in the absence of animals but it's not. For instance fat can be obtained from oil products and legumes and grains provide with a planned diet the same protein amount plus other vitamins and minerals. Although it looks like the top choice diet it's not simple to stick with the diet. Fast food meat such as chicken dairy and eggs cost is notably cheaper than fruits and vegetables. And what's more it's very difficult to find a vegan restaurant or a vegan supermarket in a fast food culture. The world learns from its mistakes and changes are the reason because we have a better world to live. That's why the vegan diet is the result of many search tries of a pure diet which goal is to avoid the most frequent diseases caused because a poor nutrition and the violation of the animal rights.
