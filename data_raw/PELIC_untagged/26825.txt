According to the authors children lose their interests in learning within three or four years after they start going to school because the school system tries to tailor the children into its own strict rules curricula schedules etc. The author claims that all of these factors existing in the current education system prevent children from showing and developing their creativity thus calling them creativity killers. They are itemized in the passage as surveillance evaluation rewards competition over control restricting choice pressure and time. Nd did very well. Going to further schools I was always one of best students and was appreciated by my teachers. However I knew that I lost my creativity a lot while I was trying to learn only what I was taught. Whenever I found a friend who did not do better than me in school but who was making his or her own way full of ideas and authenticities I always felt both jealousy and admiration.
