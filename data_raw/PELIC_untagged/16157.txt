Oakland is going to build two hotels. In the past few years lots of parents want to attend children's graduation ceremony however hotels in Oakland are limited. In order to make parents convenient and prevent that parents can't have a hotel Oakland decided to have two new hotels. They will have a new one on Boulevard of the Allies which will have 200 to 250 rooms. The other one will be on Bayard Street which will have seven stories with parking garage. It will be very convenient for those parents want to visit universities and attend children's graduation.
