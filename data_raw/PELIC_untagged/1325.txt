Air pollution is one of the most important problems nowadays that everyone is involved in. All of us must try as much as he can to protect our earth and environment. There are a lot of solutions to help in solving the problem. Establishing a good public transportation will let the people for example use it and avoid using their own cars to go work or to go places where it is very difficult to find a parking. So by using the public transportation the problem may be solved because the people will use less gas which is one of the very dangers problems which is involved in the air pollution problem.
