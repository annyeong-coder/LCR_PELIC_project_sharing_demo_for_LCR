There are a lot of different countries in the world. Every country has its own characteristic and peculiarity. These characteristic and uniqueness can be reflected on its traditional food. All country has its own staple food. Korea has its unique food. The food can be evaluated by nutrition taste and time to cook Korean food. Korean food is good for health because almost Korean food is consisted of vegetables and grains. Nutritionally it has fewer calories than fast food. So if people eat same amount of Korean food and fast food Korean food is less fattening and has much nutrition. Korean food has many vitamins because there are a lot of vegetables. Also vitamins are good for intestines and constipation so people who have problems in their intestines are hardly seen in Korea. Korean food has very different tastes because there are a lot of cuisines and sauces. Almost fast food tastes oily pungent and usually the tastes are almost same among fast food restaurants. Also most fast food is often fried food. But Korean food can be spicy sour sweet and tasted like roasted sesame and it can be steamed fried boiled and parched. The sauces are also made by vegetables. So Korean food is hardly become fed up with because of its variety tastes. Korean people call Korean food slow food contrasting with fast food because it takes long time to cook comparing with fast food. There are several steps to cook it. It requires many hands but usually Korean people do not omit those steps because they think that they have to do everything with sincerity. Even though Korean food takes long time to cook the overall evaluation of Korean food is quite good in terms of nutrition and taste.
