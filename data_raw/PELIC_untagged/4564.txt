Quality of a good doctor study hard sacrifice their quality of life think of their patient know new information about medicine Qualities of a good doctor include many factors. First of all they have to study hard. They have to study not only when they are medical students but also after graduation. The world of medicine is always changing. To perform the best therapy for their patients they need to try to know new information about medicine. Second they have to think of their patients all the time. Even if they are at home on holiday they must not forget their patients. If the condition of patients are changed they will go to see them. Eventually they are forced to sacrifice their own life. Even on weekend they cannot be relaxed with their family. Their patients should be the most important thing for the doctors. Finally they should be healthy. The most important thing for doctors to work everyday is not getting sick easily. They are working all the day and sometimes they have to keep working during the night.
