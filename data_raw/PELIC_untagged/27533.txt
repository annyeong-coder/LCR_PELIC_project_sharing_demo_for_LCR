The text talks about a psychic who can find missing people. The main idea is that a psychic Annette Martin A. M. Helps policemen find missing people by using her special power. There are two real cases as evidence. First A. M. Needs the picture of the victim to experience the progress of being attacked by her special power then she can find the missing people and she does. Second A. M. Predicts that the policemen can arrest the suspect after one year after approximately one year her precognition really comes true. The special power what A. M. Has is undoubted by some people but it also doubted by some people.
