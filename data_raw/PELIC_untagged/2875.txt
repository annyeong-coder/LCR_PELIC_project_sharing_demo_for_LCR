Subculture In Syria a lot of farmers work out side the house. Many of them can not read or write because they do not have time to go to school. They just want to work and have money but usually most of them are intelligent. Most of them have a small house. For example their houses contain just living room bathroom kitchen and small bedroom. Usually most of them get up early. For example some of them get up at 5 00 a. M then they eat the breakfast and walk to work about one hour or more. Often many of them do not have expensive clothes. For instance they dress up casual clothes.
