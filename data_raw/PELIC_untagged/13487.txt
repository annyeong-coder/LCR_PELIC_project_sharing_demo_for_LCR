Dating in group from Altoarg When the couple in age between 16 and more they go out the camp as group boys and girls in the summer. When the moon is complete. And they start singing and telling stories and shouting. At that time if any boy loves any girl he tells her that. After that they tell them parents and the will get marriage soon. Before they get marriage they can have dating as much as they like in same place with the same group . But with out payment because there is no restaurant in the desert .
