It is not difficult to make a bookcase when you follow these steps. The first step you need to prepare wood nails glue a hammer a saw sand paper and paint. Next is the most important step. You have to cut wood exactly 2 sidepieces 11 by 28 3 pieces for top bottom and shelf 11 by 13 by 28 and two 10 molding strips. Then you should sand each piece of wood down. After that you ought to glue one molding strip to each side piece 14 down from top. From then on you should nail side pieces to bottom and nail the top in place. The next step you may nail the back piece in place. After that you can paint the bookcase and shelf. Then you let dry 2 hours. Finally you can slide the shelf into place.
