CNN reported that the price of a gallon of gasoline has jumped back up near 3 a gallon according to a survey published Sunday. The Lundberg Survey found that the average price for a gallon of self serve regular was 2. 96 On Friday up 16 cents from the previous survey two weeks earlier. If crude oil prices didn't retreat gas prices could easily cross back over the 3 mark on average said publisher Trilby Lundberg. It's said that prices had reached an all time high of 3. 18 In May. The current price 2. 96 Was 78 cents higher than it had been one year ago Lundberg said. CNN reported that the survey looked at thousands of gas stations across the country. It had found the highest average price for a gallon of self serve regular was in San Francisco at 3. 28. The lowest was in Newark New Jersey at 2. 73.
