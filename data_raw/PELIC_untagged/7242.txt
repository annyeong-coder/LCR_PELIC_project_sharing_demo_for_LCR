In information age liberal arts have made it important for students to comprehend many subjects to study their major and their work. One example of this is that psychology is not only comprehension for human mind but also analysis for human behavior. A sales person who was allowed not to study psychology and studied only economics in university might not be able to function in human requirement. He might suggest clients unnecessary products and might lose their credit. Moreover surely scientists benefit from having taken exercise class. Both exercising and performing laboratory experiments require a balance between technical control and imagination. Playing baseball might refer to analyzing air stream because thinking about batting is required to reduce air resistance. Therefore it is important for students to acquire core part of many subjects for their major.
