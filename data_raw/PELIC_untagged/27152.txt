Drugs Addiction In today's world medicine is available in pharmacies. If people are sick they can buy some patent medicine to take. Due to technology improvement medicines save human beings from fatal diseases. However recently drugs abusing becomes a serious problem. Especially some drugs such as heroin or amphetamine have lethal ingredients that can kill people. Drugs addiction affects people health increases the crime rate and influences addicts' relationships. Drugs addiction has deeply effects on people physical and mental health. Many people think that more medicine makes them healthier. In fact every drug has its dosage and usage. If people use them in wrong way they may be harmful to physical health. Furthermore overtaking drugs will decrease addicts' appetite and hurt their brains. Some drugs make them have illusion during the medicine efficacy. After the drug efficacy is over they may be absent minded. They probably lose their jobs because of mental problems. The drugs addiction problem also associates with crime. People who are suffering from this problem will have a strong desire to take more drugs. Most of them have no money to support their desires therefore they use improper methods to get money. They may steal goods from stores to exchange cash or rob banks. Addicts who are like walking bombs threaten their families and friends. While they are going through withdraw they may be easily out of control. This leads to they develop violence tendency to their families and friends. There is a lot of news about abusers hurt their parents since they cannot ask for money from parents. Even they kill their friends but they don't remember when they lose their minds. Drug addiction cause many problems among influencing people physically and mentally increasing crime and breaking abusers' relationships. Medicine can save people or kill people that depend on how to use them. As a result we should put more focus on drugs addiction problems.
