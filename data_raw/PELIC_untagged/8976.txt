On the contrary another gender suicide is male. The rate of male suicide falls slowly from 2001 to 2002. Then the rate of male suicide rises slowly. If we compare between the rate of male suicide and the rate of female suicide we find that the average rate of male suicide are higher than the average rate of female suicide.
