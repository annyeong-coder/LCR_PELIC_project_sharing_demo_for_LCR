Diego Rivera is considered to be one of the three greatest muralist in Mexico. The Bellas Artes palace in Mexico city is decoreded with his murals. Diego Rivera murals are known aroun the world. Native culture are reflected in his work. The land a farmer and a working class are considered the principal topics of his work. La Creacion is considered the first mural that he painted. He is regarded as one of the founded of the communist party in Mexico. He is considered as one of the artist very interested in the political situation of the country.
