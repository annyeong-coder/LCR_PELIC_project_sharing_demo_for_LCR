It is easy to find articles about your topic if you know what you find and where you go. First of all you must know the best place where you can find necessary document and information. And then you must know how to use the computerized card catalog. It is really quite easy when you type in your topic on computer and press the return key. The next step the computer will search its database of magazines and journals and give you a list about your topic. Besides this program will give you a brief summary of the article. The last step if you want to learn where the magazines and journals are and how to use them you must go to the periodical section on the second floor. But if you need them you can only make a copy instead of taking them out. Finally while you find articles about your topic you must go to the best place and use the computerized catalog.
