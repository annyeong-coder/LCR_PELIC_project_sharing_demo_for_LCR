Almost all report of diabetes in kids concentrate on Type2 diabetes. However recently the incidence of new cases of Type1 diabetes in European and U. S. Children is increasing much faster than the past. Type 1 diabetes is that the body cannot produce enough insulin to control blood sugar levels. If it is too high that can affect on the eyes heart kidneys and some organs. There are unusual symptoms showing Type1 diabetes such as extreme hunger or thirst blurry vision and fatigue. Children facing with Type1 diabetes must learn how to check and control their blood sugar levels to live in normal and healthy life.
